# Report: 我的画像 (Self-Portrait)

### Summary
"我的画像" refers to the continuous, iterative construction of self-awareness and personal identity grounded in the principles of self-observation, emotional mastery, and authenticity. It is not a fixed image but a dynamic state that evolves through empirical trial, reflective practice, and the courageous acceptance of both strengths and limitations. By integrating internal values with external reality, this portrait serves as a foundational guide for navigating life with conscious intentionality.

### Key Findings
*   **Dynamic Construction**: Identity is formed through "Bayesian Thinking for the Soul"—constantly refining internal frameworks based on new experiences, successes, and failures, rather than seeking absolute, permanent certainty.
*   **The Three Stages of Vision (见自己，见世界，见众生)**: Progressing from deep self-understanding (knowing strengths/limitations) to social awareness (understanding trends/rules) and finally to altruistic influence (helping others).
*   **Emotional & Projection Mastery**: Recognizing that reactions to others—whether attraction or repulsion—often mirror unintegrated parts of one's own internal landscape.
*   **Conscious Architecture**: Prioritizing truth, independence of mind, and the courage to act according to internal truth rather than external validation.
*   **Productive Life-Design**: The principle of "Production over Consumption" (80/20 rule) is essential for maintaining creativity and long-term personal growth.

### Supporting Evidence
Evidence is derived from the foundational framework of [[Self-Knowledge-and-Personal-Growth]], supported by ongoing reflective practices documented in [[The-Log-Of-Your-Life]] and various diary entries (e.g., `raw/diary/self/2017-11-07-upgrade-my-self.md`, `raw/diary/notes/2023-06-13-on-managing-yourself.md`). These sources emphasize the transition from reactive living to conscious existence through techniques such as Feedback Analysis and the utilization of the "Socratic Mirror."
