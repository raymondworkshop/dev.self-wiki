朋友只看私德，不看大众评价。

**[AI Synthesis]**

*   **Private Virtue (私德)**: This is the domain of genuine, sustained relationships where character is tested under conditions of intimacy and vulnerability. It requires consistent, unperformative integrity.
*   **Public Opinion (大众评价)**: This is a diffuse, often transient metric based on performance or visibility. While useful for career navigation, it is insufficient for judging core character.

**[Principle/Mental Model]**

*   **The Filter of Proximity**: The quality of one's character is most rigorously tested and judged by those with whom one shares deep proximity. Their assessment forms the most reliable metric of self-worth and relational capacity.

**## Evolution**

*   *Initial thought:* Public perception matters for success. 
*   *Revised Principle:* Public perception is a variable of the external environment; private virtue is the invariant of the self.
