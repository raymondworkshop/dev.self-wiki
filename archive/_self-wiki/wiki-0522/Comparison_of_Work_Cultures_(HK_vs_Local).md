> The original Cantonese phrase suggests that regardless of the current situation, a certain standard or expectation should be maintained or upheld. This implies a cultural context where professional competence is expected to be inherent rather than needing external validation.

### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/origin-apple-notes/广东话-鼓励 gu2 lai6.md]]
The phrase '鼓励 gu2 lai6' carries the weight of an established cultural expectation regarding professional execution. It suggests that the baseline standard of performance should be maintained irrespective of immediate circumstances or external pressures. This contrasts with contexts where deference or excessive validation might be sought.

### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/origin-apple-notes/内地 关系 很重要，尤其是熟人之间.md]]
The concept of 'Guanxi' (关系) is noted as being highly significant in the mainland context, especially among known individuals. This suggests that transactional success in that environment is deeply interwoven with pre-existing social capital and trust, which differs from transactional models elsewhere.

## 香港人 (Hong Kong People)
*   **Characteristics**: 機敏 (Keen/Astute), 多趨利 (Pragmatic/Opportunistic). 
*   **Underlying Value**: Despite pragmatism, they possess principles and acknowledge universal values. 
*   **Modern Context**: While many capable Hong Kong individuals face bleak prospects due to political pressure and the erosion of the 'One Country, Two Systems' framework, those who move elsewhere pursue opportunities, which is seen as a characteristic of the people.

## 中国人 (The Other Group)
*   **Development Trajectory**: Possesses inherent kindness but matures slowly, often catalyzed by those who are more astute. 
*   **Contrast**: When others are quick-witted, they may appear slow; when others weigh pros and cons, they are sincere; when others are calculating, they achieve spiritual clarity.
*   **Outcome**: Though they eventually gain clarity, they remain fundamentally kind and sincere, constantly seeking like-minded individuals but often ending in isolation.
