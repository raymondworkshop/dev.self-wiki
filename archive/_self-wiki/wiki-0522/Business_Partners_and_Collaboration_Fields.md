> This concept outlines a partnership model based on shared risk and mutual benefit, suggesting a foundation for robust business relationships. It emphasizes that successful collaboration requires alignment on both potential upside and downside.



### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/origin-apple-notes/business todo.md]]
**Principle of Open Collaboration:**
The operational model for business partnership must be founded upon the principle of shared risk and shared benefit (以风险共担、利益共享的模式).

**Application:** This framework applies to establishing new ventures or deepening existing relationships where mutual investment is required.
