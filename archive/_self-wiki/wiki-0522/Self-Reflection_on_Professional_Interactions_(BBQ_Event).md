> This reflection stems from a recent BBQ event and identifies key areas requiring personal development. The primary areas needing improvement identified are social skills and linguistic proficiency in professional settings.



### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/origin-apple-notes/今天去了BBQ, 需要提高的地方.md]]
**Areas for Improvement Identified:**
1. Social Skills: Specifically, the tendency to omit personal details like age, relying solely on professional titles when interacting with high-level contacts (e.g., those from large corporations).
2. Language Proficiency: Need for greater fluency and appropriateness in professional discourse.

**Contextual Note:** The interaction context involved high-stakes professional environments where mutual benefit ('共赢') was the underlying expectation, necessitating a higher level of social calibration.
