> This principle emphasizes the necessity of forming one's own opinions and making independent decisions without undue reliance on others. It establishes a baseline for intellectual autonomy, contrasting it with the act of considering others' viewpoints.



### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/origin-apple-notes/mindset.md]]
**Principle: Intellectual Autonomy**
- **Think for yourself**: Form your own decisions and opinions without depending on others. (Independent thought is the baseline.)
- **Think of others**: Hold an opinion regarding others' viewpoints. (Consideration of external perspectives.)
