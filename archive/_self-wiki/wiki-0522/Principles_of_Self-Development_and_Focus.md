> True selfhood involves possessing one's own thoughts, maintaining positivity, and harboring kindness. It requires finding one's unique path and utilizing one's full potential while accepting the inherent fragility of the world's ease of becoming uneasy.

### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/origin-apple-notes/self skills.md]]
**Core Tenets of Selfhood (自我认知):**
*   Possessing independent thought, health, positivity, and a kind heart is essential for sustained happiness. (有自己的思想，健康，正面，可以保持开心的感觉和善良的心)
*   Grounding oneself in reality is crucial, as the world is easily prone to anxiety or negative shifts. (觉踏实地...这个世界好容易不安)
*   Self-love is a prerequisite for growth. (要好爱自己)
*   Finding one's own method and utilizing one's potential are key to action. (要找到自己的方法 / 用自己的潜能)
*   Marriage/Partnership is defined as mutual teamwork and shared commitment. (结婚是 teamwork / 想到兩個人同一條心去付出)
*   The approach to life should be one of allowing things to unfold naturally. (let life flow)

**Cognitive Note:** The initial feeling of 'doing what feels right' must be balanced with sustained, intentional action. (觉得正确就做了)

### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/origin-apple-notes/life self.md]]
**伊顿精神的内核：努力与淡然的结合**
1. **努力上进 (Effort)**：基石在于持续的努力和进取心。
2. **淡然冷待 (Detachment)**：学会放下执念，控制情绪的表达和接收。
- **保持冷静 (Composure)**：成功避免将事务情绪化，做到客观处理。
- **不自我惩罚 (Non-Self-Blame)**：不将他人的过错内化为自身的罪过。
- **选择性行动 (Agency)**：面对冒犯，主体拥有选择权——可以选择法律途径、选择不予理睬，或选择和解。关键在于认知到“冒犯者的问题在于他，而非在我”。

**[AI Synthesis]**：此原则强调了主体在互动中的能动性（Agency）。情绪的反应是可控的，而非被动的受害者状态。当外部刺激出现时，主体需将其视为一个可供选择的输入信号，而非不可抗拒的命运。

## Socratic Summary
This raw data outlines a comprehensive framework for personal mastery, linking physical, emotional, and intellectual health to achieving a 'Great' self. Key themes include the necessity of intentionality, accepting responsibility, and understanding that true confidence stems from an objective acceptance of one's authentic self.

### Core Pillars of Mastery (Goal: Be a Great Man)

**1. Foundational Health (The Base Layer):**
*   **Physical:** Maintain fitness, diet, and appearance (dress well). Body health is foundational to mental clarity.
*   **Emotional:** Master emotions by externalizing pressure ('Write it down, distinguish truth from fiction'). The key to quieting internal narrative ('内心戏') is awareness.
*   **Mental/Intellectual:** Enhance language ability; express oneself more clearly.

**2. Mindset & Self-Perception (The Operating System):**
*   **Confidence:** Defined as having a fitting, objective view of oneself—a complete acceptance of the real self. Self-respect is true confidence.
*   **Responsibility:** Attitude shift; no victim mentality. Ownership of actions is paramount.
*   **Intention:** Intention dictates action. Unclear intention breeds significant distress.
*   **Vulnerability & Trust:** Building strong relationships requires initial vulnerability (e.g., '先背叛' - initial trust/risk) and operating in a Win-Win framework.

**3. Action & Execution (The Output Layer):**
*   **Prioritization:** Organize the day around 3-4 'Big' priorities (Mission/Work, Fitness, Family/Social Life).
*   **Independence & Accountability:** Optimize for independence; be accountable for your output, not just your input. Ask for help when truly needed to show humility and openness.
*   **Courage & Risk:** Take calculated risks. Do not play zero-sum games; seek infinite games where value can be created for all parties.

**4. Deeper Philosophical Synthesis (The Meta-Layer):**
*   **Self-Love:** Loving the self as it is ('She loves me for who I am') reduces stress and increases genuine presence.
*   **Growth Mindset:** Be open to feedback and growth, but ground efforts in the knowledge that 'I can do better.'
*   **The Role of Others:** Cherish few, authentic friends whose presence offers intelligence, energy, and integrity. Recognize that cutting ties with old patterns is the cost of growth.

### TODOs & Refinements
*   **Daily Focus:** Align daily actions with the 'Big 3 or 4' priorities.
*   **Emotional Boundary:** Do not give away your emotions for free. Use writing to validate or discard emotional pressure.
*   **Pursuit:** The ultimate goal is to be a 'Great' individual through the integration of Health, Love, and Mission.
