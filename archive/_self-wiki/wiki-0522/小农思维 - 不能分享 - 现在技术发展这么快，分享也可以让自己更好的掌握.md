> This note reflects a tension between traditional, localized approaches ('smallholder mindset') and the modern imperative of sharing knowledge in a rapidly evolving technological landscape. The core insight is that sharing must be strategic; it is a tool for mastery, not merely an act of dissemination.



### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/origin-apple-notes/sharing.md]]
The initial sentiment suggests a reluctance to share, rooted in a 'smallholder' mentality, which contrasts with the modern context. However, the note pivots to suggest that in the current era of rapid technological advancement, sharing knowledge is not a loss but a method to achieve deeper, collective mastery. This requires a shift from viewing knowledge as a finite resource to viewing it as a multiplier.
