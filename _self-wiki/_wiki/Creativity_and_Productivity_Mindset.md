> This document synthesizes philosophies on maintaining creative output, achieving excellence, and structuring knowledge for high-leverage application. It contrasts the pursuit of mere activity with the pursuit of meaningful, high-quality contribution.
>
> The core tension lies between the need for deep, focused execution and the broader philosophical pursuit of a life well-lived through meaningful creation.

## Evolution
- Initial thoughts were scattered across various domains (SaaS, education, art, business).
- The synthesis has focused on establishing a unified mindset: excellence requires both deep technical execution and a philosophical grounding in the *why* and *how* of creation.

## Content

### 1. The Pursuit of Excellence (The "Why" and "How")
*   **Life Must Be Felt, Not Observed:** True fulfillment comes from actively engaging in the task and bringing one's knowledge to bear through action, not merely observing life or intellectualizing it. (Source: [[self-wiki/raw/diary/philosophy/2019-05-28-keep-my-creativity.md]])
*   **Excellence as the Goal:** Happiness stems from the full use of one's power to achieve excellence in one's chosen domain. This requires bringing one's knowledge to bear through active participation. (Source: [[self-wiki/raw/diary/philosophy/2019-05-28-keep-my-creativity.md]])
*   **The Role of Philosophy:** While deep philosophical inquiry is valuable, the practical application must be guided by *how* to execute, not getting lost in *why* in a way that paralyzes action. One must ask "How?" more than "Why?" to avoid becoming a mere theorist. (Source: [[self-wiki/raw/diary/philosophy/2019-05-28-keep-my-creativity.md]])

### 2. Maintaining Peak Performance (The "How to Stay Sharp")
*   **Growth through Tension:** Peak performance is maintained through a dynamic balance: **Moderate Pressure + Moderate Rest (Switching)**. Stagnation occurs without challenge. (Source: [[self-wiki/raw/diary/philosophy/2019-05-28-keep-my-creativity.md]])
*   **The Power of Struggle:** Significant breakthroughs often occur *after* a period of difficult struggle. Initial assistance is needed, but the deepest learning happens when one pushes through discomfort. (Source: [[self-wiki/raw/diary/philosophy/2019-05-28-keep-my-creativity.md]])
*   **Deliberate Practice:** To advance, one must engage in **Deep Focus** on specific, challenging targets. This means dedicating time to a task while setting goals slightly beyond current capability, even if the process is uncomfortable. (Source: [[self-wiki/raw/diary/philosophy/2019-05-28-keep-my-creativity.md]])

### 3. From Idea to Product (The Execution Layer)
*   **The Product Manager Mindset:** A successful product contributor must move beyond mere idea generation. They must be able to lead the entire lifecycle—planning, designing, developing, and marketing—to create something profitable for the company. (Source: [[self-wiki/raw/diary/philosophy/2019-05-28-keep-my-creativity.md]])
*   **The Essence of Creativity:** Creativity is the ability to take existing knowledge/concepts, fully understand them, and translate them into a novel, visualized expression. This requires **Imagination (Uniqueness) + Synthesis (Coherence) + Professional Skill (Execution)**. (Source: [[self-wiki/raw/diary/philosophy/2019-05-28-keep-my-creativity.md]])
*   **System Thinking:** All endeavors should be approached with systemic thinking (e.g., DRY principle, reversibility) to ensure scalability and maintainability. (Source: [[self-wiki/raw/diary/philosophy/2019-05-28-keep-my-creativity.md]])
