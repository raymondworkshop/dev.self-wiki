> This entry synthesizes the goals, activities, and reflections from the end of July 2021. It covers planning for the remainder of the month across career pursuits, personal skill development, and relationship management. The entries reflect a period of intense focus on self-improvement and practical application of learned concepts.

## Evolution
- This initial entry serves as a baseline snapshot of the individual's goals and activities during July 2021. Future entries will build upon this foundation, showing how these goals were pursued, adjusted, or abandoned.

## Content

### 2021-07-07 Review
*   **Reflection**: Enjoyed the meetup and the conversation with a female student. The key takeaway was the need to allow women to speak about themselves and to avoid unproductive conversations ("don't chat more with losers").
*   **Goals**: Review the driver's textbook and take the driving test.
*   **Improvement Area**: Waking up at 10:30 AM.

### July Planning (July 2021 - July 31)
*   **Budget**: Allocated 4200 HKD, with 600 HKD designated for investment.
*   **Dating**: Planned a hiking date with Ivy, aiming to build a pleasurable habit and deepen understanding through "Womanese 101."
*   **Career**: Focused on being an entrepreneur (pursuing "How to Get Rich") and developing engineering skills ("How to Design Programs"), while also considering the role of a follower.
*   **Habit Improvement**:
    *   Sleep: Aim to sleep before 11:30 PM and wake up before 8:00 AM.
    *   Hygiene: Practice brushing teeth before bed.
*   **Skills Development**:
    *   Stretch: Practice and deepen knowledge on stretching, alongside reading "Keys to Drawing" by Bert Dodson.
    *   Learning How to Learn: Reading "Mastery" by George Leonard.
    *   Language: Practicing English and Cantonese.
    *   Self-Improvement: Maintaining self-belief and acting as a value-giver.

### Subsequent Entries (July 28 - August 10)
*   **July 28**: Focused on reading "Conversation Casanova," thinking about the future (wealth, family, health), and self-analysis regarding building something beneficial to society.
*   **July 30**: Focused on thinking about the future regarding career, family, and wealth.
*   **August 2-10**: The focus shifted heavily towards practical application, including reading "How to Design Program," updating technical files (`train.json`, `test.json`), and reflecting on the need to align current efforts with future goals. A recurring theme was the need to think about one's future and the current professional situation.
