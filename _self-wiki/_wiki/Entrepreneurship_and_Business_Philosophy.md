## Content

### 创业生意经 (Business Acumen)

*   **从小生意做起，从小问题做起 (Start small, start with small problems):** The journey begins with a focused, manageable problem to solve.
*   **想办法解决难题 (Find ways to solve difficult problems):** The core of business is finding solutions. The willingness to execute necessary steps to reach a goal is paramount.
*   **先迈出一步，迈出以后，再看怎样修正自己的错误 (Take the first step; correct errors afterward):** Action precedes perfection. Trying bravely leaves no regret, and iteration is part of the process.
*   **充分的成本控制 (Adequate Cost Control):** Financial discipline is crucial for survival.
*   **赢得顾客的信任 (Winning Customer Trust):** This is achieved by proactively addressing customer concerns and demonstrating tangible value to the client.
*   **用大钱生小钱 (Making small money from big money):** This requires self-restraint in spending and the accumulation of capital for strategic investment.
*   **持续拓展生意的渠道 (Continuously Expanding Business Channels):** Growth requires diversifying business models and markets.
