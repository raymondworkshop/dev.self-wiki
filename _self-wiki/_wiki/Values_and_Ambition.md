> True ambition lies not in the titles or external validation we chase, but in the rigorous forging and integrity of our internal values. We must be ambitious in *what* we value, rather than in the transient symbols of success.

## Evolution
- Initial concept derived from a specific piece of advice regarding ambition vs. attachment to status.

## Content
The core message is: "我们要知道自己想要什么，但不要去贪恋光环，而是看重对自己价值观的锻造。" (We must know what we want, but not become attached to the halo/glory; rather, we must value the forging of our own values.) This is encapsulated by the maxim: "Be ambitious in your values, not in your titles."
