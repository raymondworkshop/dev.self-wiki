> This concept revolves around the intentional elevation of execution quality, moving beyond mere completion to achieving a level of sophistication and thoughtfulness in how things are done. It suggests that the *manner* of execution carries as much weight as the outcome itself.

## Evolution
- Initial conceptualization of the need for higher standards in output and process.
