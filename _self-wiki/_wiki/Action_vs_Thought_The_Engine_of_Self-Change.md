**Core Tenets (Distillation of [[origin-apple-notes/insight people.md]]):**
1. **Action Over Theory:** 行动，而不是坐而论道 (Action, not just theorizing).
2. **Drivers of Growth:** Curiosity and diligence are the necessary fuels for learning and forward movement.
3. **Presence:** Maintaining acute sensitivity to current realities is crucial for effective engagement with life and self-improvement.

*(Note: This content serves as a foundational reminder against analysis paralysis and inaction.)*

### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/origin-apple-notes/相信 soeng1 seon3.md]]
The raw text presents a sequence: Belief/Acceptance (相信), followed by the need for vigorous effort (发奋), timely execution (及时), and the ultimate goal of achieving a profound outcome (抱降急极记). This mirrors the cognitive gap between conceptual understanding and applied reality.

**Distillation Focus:** The passage emphasizes that mere belief is insufficient; it must be coupled with immediate, high-energy action to yield significant results. The sequence suggests a critical path from acceptance to execution.

### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/diary/language/2020-06-08-business-english.md]]
**Growth Mindset (Praxis):** Progression is defined as the combination of critical thinking, reflection, and action. This cycle involves: 1. Plan and Action (Executing the plan and considering impacts). 2. Reflect (Analyzing the results of the action). 3. Improve (Altering and revising conceptions based on reflection). 4. Plan and Action Again (Implementing revised plans). 

**Courage vs. Confidence:** Courage is an internal quality required both to speak up and to sit down and listen. Confidence, conversely, is an external manifestation.
