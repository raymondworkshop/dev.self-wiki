## Evolution

## Content
### Execution Philosophy: Speed vs. Perfection
The principle exemplified by Musk's approach suggests that in certain contexts, velocity of deployment ("先发射") must precede perfection ("后升级"). This prioritizes market feedback and iteration over achieving a flawless initial product.

### Leadership and Following Dynamics
Leadership is situational and relational:
*   **Lead the people who need you.** (Situational leadership)
*   **Follow the people who you need.** (Situational followership)

The capacity to lead or follow requires commensurate intelligence and wisdom:
*   **Be smart enough to lead.**
*   **Be wise enough to follow.**

### Personal Action vs. Overthinking
To achieve clarity and success, intellectual paralysis must be overcome through action:
*   To stop overthinking, take more action.
*   Clear thinking combined with knowing what you want constitutes a form of success.
