> The principle of diligent effort is paramount, recognizing that effortless gains are often not freely given. Furthermore, maintaining boundaries by refusing to engage in unproductive arguments with those unwilling to discuss matters seriously is a crucial aspect of self-preservation.

## Evolution
- Initial thoughts centered on the inherent value of hard work and the transactional nature of effort. This has evolved to include the equally important cognitive skill of knowing *when* and *with whom* to apply that effort.

## Content
### The Value of Effort
Effort is not inherently free; it requires investment, and this investment must be acknowledged. (Source: [[self-wiki/raw/origin-apple-notes/努力工作.md]])

### Boundary Setting in Discourse
It is vital to refuse to argue with individuals who are unwilling to engage in a meaningful discussion. This protects mental energy and focuses effort where it yields return. (Source: [[self-wiki/raw/origin-apple-notes/努力工作.md]])
