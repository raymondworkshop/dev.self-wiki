### 💪 力量训练 (Strength Training)

**背阔肌 (Latissimus Dorsi) - 滑轮下拉 (Cable Pulldown)**
- 肩胛骨向下 (Scapulae depression)
- 10下/4组 (10 reps/4 sets)

**肱三头肌 (Triceps) - 伸展 (Extension)**
- 保持站姿，双脚与肩同宽 (Maintain standing posture, feet shoulder-width apart)
- 双手拿起哑铃，将双臂向上举过头顶 (Hold dumbbells, raise arms overhead)
- 弯曲肘部，肩膀、手肘、手腕保持在同一条直线上，慢慢将哑铃放下至脑后 (Bend elbows, keep shoulder, elbow, and wrist in a straight line, slowly lower dumbbells behind the head)
- 回到起始位置，并重复动作 (Return to starting position and repeat)

**胸肌 (Pectorals) - 平凳哑铃卧推 (Flat Dumbbell Bench Press)**
- 躺在平凳上，双脚平放在地面，保持稳定 (Lie on a flat bench, feet flat on the ground, maintain stability).
- 双手各持一个哑铃，放在胸部两侧，掌心向前 (Hold one dumbbell in each hand, placed at sides of chest, palms facing forward).
- 吸气，收紧核心肌群，稳定身体 (Inhale, contract core muscles, stabilize body).
- 同时将哑铃向上推，直到手臂几乎伸直，但不要完全锁死肘关节 (Simultaneously press dumbbells upward until arms are almost straight, but do not fully lock the elbow).
- 呼气，慢慢将哑铃降低回到起始位置，保持动作控制，不要让哑铃掉落 (Exhale, slowly lower dumbbells back to the starting position, maintaining control, do not let them drop).
