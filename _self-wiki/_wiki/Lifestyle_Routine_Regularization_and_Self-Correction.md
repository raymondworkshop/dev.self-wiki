> This entry reflects on the need for establishing consistent lifestyle habits, specifically regarding sleep and wake times, as a necessary foundation for sustained personal development. The pursuit of excessive freedom and spontaneity, while valued, has incurred a measurable cost that requires recalibration.

### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/diary/diary/2016-07-15-weekly-summary.md]]
**生活规律化方面 (Areas for Improvement):**
*   **作息纪律 (Routine):** Must establish a stricter schedule; aiming to be present by 10 PM and asleep before 1 AM.
*   **自由度的代价 (Cost of Freedom):** Acknowledgment that the pursuit of excessive freedom and spontaneity has exacted a tangible cost that must now be managed.
