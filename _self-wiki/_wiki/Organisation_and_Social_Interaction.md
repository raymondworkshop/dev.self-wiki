## Content
### Organizing Activities with Strangers
The suggested sequence for organizing an activity involving strangers is:
1. Publish the activity information.
2. Meet up.
3. Conduct the activity.
4. Take photos.
5. Share a meal.

### Core Principles
*   **Mutual Aid ($\text{互助}$)**: The foundation of the gathering.
*   **Respect for the Individual ($\text{尊重个人}$)**: Valuing each participant's presence and boundaries.
