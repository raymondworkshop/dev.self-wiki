> The role of a CEO can be distilled into three fundamental, high-leverage activities: assembling a superior team, ensuring perpetual financial resources, and establishing a clear organizational vision. These three pillars—people, capital, and direction—are interdependent and critical for scaling any venture.

## Content
### The Three Pillars of CEO Success

A CEO's primary focus should remain on these three areas:

1.  **Team Building:** Find and cultivate a truly exceptional team. (Focus on *People*)
2.  **Financial Sustainability:** Never allow the organization to run out of money; instead, focus on generating and compounding capital. (Focus on *Capital*)
3.  **Vision Setting:** Define and maintain the overarching vision and goals for the company. (Focus on *Direction*)
