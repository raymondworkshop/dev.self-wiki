> This page synthesizes reflections on the necessary skills and commitments required for personal and professional advancement, tracking the journey toward achieving higher standards. It documents the milestones needed to move from foundational learning to advanced execution across various domains.

## Evolution
- Initial entries focused on immediate, tangible goals (e.g., passing exams, learning skills).
- Later entries have broadened this to encompass holistic life mastery, integrating technical skills with soft skills and mindset shifts.

## Content
### Core Commitments for Advancement
The path to achieving higher standards requires dedication across several vectors:

1.  **Academic/Professional Validation:** Achieving a high-quality output, such as a publishable paper, signifies reaching a recognized standard of competence.
2.  **Skill Acquisition (Physical/Technical):** Mastery of physical skills (e.g., driving, swimming) alongside cognitive skills (e.g., advanced English proficiency) forms a holistic base.
3.  **Linguistic Competence:** Proficiency in target languages (English, Cantonese) is crucial for broader intellectual exchange.

### Immediate Goals (As of 2016-06-28)
*   Achieve formal acceptance/establishment in a current pursuit ("终于上了正规").
*   Produce a high-quality academic paper.
*   Master driving and swimming.
*   Achieve fluency in English and Cantonese.
*   Pass necessary language examinations.
