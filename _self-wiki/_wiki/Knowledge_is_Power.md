> True improvement stems not merely from possessing knowledge, but from the rigorous practice and application of that knowledge in reality. Furthermore, Emotional Intelligence is defined as the capacity to manage one's own emotions and those of others effectively.

## Content
### Knowledge Application vs. Possession
Knowledge itself is inert; its value is unlocked only through practical execution.
> (Source: [[self-wiki/raw/origin-apple-notes/important lesson.md]])

### Emotional Intelligence (EQ)
EQ encompasses two critical skills:
1. Self-management: The ability to regulate one's own emotional state.
2. Interpersonal Management: The capacity to accurately perceive and influence the emotions of others.
> (Source: [[self-wiki/raw/origin-apple-notes/important lesson.md]])

### Relationship Dynamics (Love/Connection)
In relationships, successful navigation requires:
- Offering sincere praise and affirmation.
- Utilizing humor as a tool to diffuse difficulties.
- Respecting and granting necessary individual space to partners.
