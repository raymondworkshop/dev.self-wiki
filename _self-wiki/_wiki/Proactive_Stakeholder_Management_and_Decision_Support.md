> Maintaining regular and proactive communication with leaders is crucial for project success and personal alignment. This involves not just reporting status but actively soliciting constructive feedback to course-correct and demonstrate ownership.

## Evolution
- Initial thoughts focused on the necessity of upward management, evolving into a structured principle of proactive feedback seeking.

## Content
### Proactive Communication Protocol
1. **Rhythmicity**: Establish a predictable cadence for check-ins (e.g., daily, weekly, milestone-based). This removes ambiguity about when input is needed.
2. **Proactivity**: Do not wait for the leader to ask for updates. Present information clearly, framing it in terms of decisions needed or risks identified.
3. **Feedback Seeking**: Frame requests for feedback not as "Is this okay?", but as "Based on X, Y, Z, my recommendation is A. Does this align with your view?" This elevates the discussion from status report to strategic partnership.
