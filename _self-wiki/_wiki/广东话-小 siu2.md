## Content
- **舅妈 (kau5 maa1)**: Aunt's wife (maternal uncle's wife).
- **姨 (yi4)**: Aunt (maternal aunt).
- **政治 (zing3 zi6)**: Politics.
- **反抗 (faan2 kong3)**: Resistance.
- **公立 (gung1 lap6)**: Public/Government-run.
- **理性 (lei5 sing3)**: Rationality.
- **排 (paai4)**: To rank/line up.
- **老师 (lou5 si1)**: Teacher.
- **竞争 (ging6 zang1)**: Competition.
- **神秘 (san4 bei3)**: Mysterious.
- **觉得 (gok3 dak1)**: To feel/think.
- **旧 (gau6)**: Old.
- **流行 (lau4 hang4)**: Fashion/Popular.
- **啤酒 (be1 dzau2)**: Beer.
- **橙 (caang4)**: Orange.
- **绸 (cau4)**: Silk.
- **台 (toi4)**: Platform/Island.
- **拆 (caak3)**: To dismantle/take apart.
- **醒目 (sing2 muk6)**: Eye-catching/Prominent.
