> A common pattern observed is that when attempting to solve issues, the tendency is to frame the problem through the lens of human interaction or psychology rather than starting with the objective facts of the situation. This bias suggests that the human element is often the default entry point into complex problem spaces.

## Evolution
- Initial observation regarding the default mode of problem engagement.

## Content
The observation is that problem resolution often defaults to a human-centric approach, whereas the initial engagement with the problem might be technical or situational.
