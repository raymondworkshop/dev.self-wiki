> The search for meaning often occurs at the intersection of the personal and the objective. While the universe may appear absurd, it is through personal investment that we forge subjective significance.
>
> ## Evolution
> - Initial concept derived from the tension between objective reality and subjective experience.

## Content
### 核心洞察 (Core Insight)
只有关己（personal），人才能荒谬中找到意义。
Nothing personal，你就无往不在荒谬之中。

### 延伸思考 (Extended Thought)
*   **荒谬的本质**: 存在本身的非理性或缺乏预设意义。
*   **意义的建构**: 意义不是被发现的，而是被建构的，它依赖于主体（自我）的投入和关怀。
