## Evolution
- This concept is being formalized from a recent raw insight, establishing a core principle for personal growth trajectory.

## Content
- The idea is that there are six opportunities (or a specific number of high-stakes scenarios) available to elevate one's current state.
- The necessity of "top-tier competitions" is highlighted as the crucible for achieving significant personal advancement.
