> Effective listening requires suspending judgment and attempting to inhabit another person's frame of reference. This deep immersion into another's viewpoint is a powerful tool for connection, even if agreement is not reached.

## Evolution
- Initial thoughts on the mechanics of deep listening, moving beyond mere hearing to genuine cognitive and emotional resonance.

## Content
The key to listening well is to experience the world through the eyes of someone else. See what they see. Feel what they feel. That doesn't mean you have to agree.
