> This outlines a two-step process for declining requests gracefully, ensuring that the refusal is cushioned by an affirmation of commitment elsewhere. It prioritizes self-preservation alongside social navigation.

## Evolution

## Content
### Declining Requests Gracefully
To say no when you dislike disappointing others:

1. Say yes to something else.
2. Inform the person your answer is no (optionally: because you said yes to something else).

'Something else' can be:
* Family
* Relationships
* Your job
* A passion
* Your mental health
* Your physical health
* Anything
