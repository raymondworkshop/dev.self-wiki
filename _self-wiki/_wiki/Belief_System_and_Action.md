> This page explores the relationship between deep-seated belief structures, the concept of God, and the necessary alignment between internal conviction and external action. It contrasts the intellectualization of belief with the transformative power of faith.

## Evolution
- Initial concepts regarding God and faith are being integrated with the need for behavioral manifestation.

## Content
### Core Concepts
**God:** The ultimate knower of hearts.
**Faith:** The cleansing agent that purifies the heart.
**Love:** A central attribute or concept related to the divine.

### Action vs. Thought
A critical distinction must be maintained between intellectualizing belief and enacting it:
> Act yourself into new thinking, don't think yourself into new acting. (Source: [[self-wiki/raw/origin-apple-notes/belief.md]])

This suggests that genuine change requires behavioral manifestation leading to cognitive shift, rather than mere rumination.
