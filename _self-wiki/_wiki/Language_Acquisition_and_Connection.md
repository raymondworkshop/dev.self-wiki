> The completion of a language course provided a realization regarding the enduring value of shared communication mediums, regardless of age. It also highlighted the ability to appropriately respond to the goodwill extended by others.

## Evolution
- Initial thoughts on language learning were purely technical; this entry adds the dimension of social and emotional payoff.

## Content
- The completion of the voice course led to the realization that there is a certain validity to what one does at a given age, primarily because it establishes a common language.
- It also involved developing the capacity to respond appropriately to the benevolent gestures of others.
