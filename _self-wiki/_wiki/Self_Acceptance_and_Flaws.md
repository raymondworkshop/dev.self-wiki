> Psychological frameworks suggest that true self-acceptance begins with acknowledging one's imperfections. Once these flaws are integrated and accepted, they lose their power to be weaponized by external criticism or internal self-doubt. This acceptance is a critical step toward emotional regulation and authentic self-possession.

## Evolution
- Initial thoughts on self-acceptance were fragmented across various notes. This page synthesizes the core idea that acceptance precedes defense.

## Content
### Psychological Integration of Flaws
The core tenet, drawn from psychological observation, is that vulnerability is a strength when paired with self-knowledge.
> "Once you’ve accepted your flaws, no one can use them against you." (Source: [[self-wiki/raw/origin-apple-notes/self learning.md]])

### Spiritual Dimension of Existence
A parallel, spiritual dimension suggests that existence itself serves a higher purpose beyond mere self-optimization.
> "我們的存在是為了 彰显神的荣耀" (Our existence is to glorify God.) (Source: [[god]])

### The Role of Feeling vs. Intellect
There is a distinction drawn between intellectualizing and experiential knowing, particularly in matters of faith or deep feeling.
> "用灵去感受神的话语，而不是用头脑去思考" (Feel God's word with the spirit, rather than thinking with the intellect.) (Source: [[god]])
> "我在祈求什么，我在感受什么" (What am I praying for? What am I feeling?) (Source: [[god]])
