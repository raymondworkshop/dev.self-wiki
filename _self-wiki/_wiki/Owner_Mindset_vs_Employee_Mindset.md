> This concept delineates two modes of engagement: working within existing structures (employee mindset) versus leading the improvement of those structures (owner mindset). The owner mindset drives exponential outcomes through strategic leverage, whereas the employee mindset tends toward linear outcomes.

## Evolution
- Initial conceptualization of roles within a system.

## Content
### Owner Mindset
- **Definition**: Working on the system itself.
- **Action**: You must possess your own system.
- **Leadership**: Be a leader whose role is to work on the system.
    - This involves deciding what the right course of action is.
    - This involves deciding what the right thing to do is to improve the system.
- **Outcomes**: Exponential.
- **Mechanism**: Leverage.

### Employee Mindset
- **Definition**: Working within the established system.
- **Outcomes**: Linear.
