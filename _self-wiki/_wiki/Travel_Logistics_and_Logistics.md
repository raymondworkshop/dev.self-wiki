> This document outlines the logistical plan for a trip to Shenzhen, covering preparation checklists, a draft itinerary, and transportation details between key locations like DaFen Oil Painting Village and Yantian Seafood Street. It serves as a practical guide for execution.

## Evolution
- Initial draft based on raw notes, now structured into a chronological itinerary with specific transport modes and timings.

## Content
### Trip Overview
**Destination:** Shenzhen
**Activities:** DaFen Oil Painting Village, DaMeiSha Seaside Walkway, Yantian Seafood Street.

### Preparation Checklist
*   Health Declaration (via WeChat)
*   Return Ticket/Macau Pass
*   Shenzhen Tong (similar to Octopus Card, available at Luohu Metro Station) or RMB

### Draft Itinerary
**Day 1:**

1.  **~10:00:** Meet at Shangshui Metro Station; Shangshui Station $\rightarrow$ Luohu Station (10 minutes).
2.  **~12:00:** DaFen Oil Painting Village. Travel: Metro Line 1 $\rightarrow$ Metro Line 3 (DaFen Station, Exit A1). (40 minutes). Allow 1 hour for leisure.
    *   **Lunch Suggestions:** Jin Feng Hong Kong Style Tea Restaurant, Japanese Cuisine, Hainan Sour Powder. (Consider Muwu BBQ - DaFen Branch).
3.  **~15:00:** Travel to Yantian Seafood Street. Travel: DaFen Oil Painting Village $\rightarrow$ DaMeiSha Seaside Park.
    *   **Transport Options:**
        *   Metro Line 3 $\rightarrow$ M380 Bus (1 hour 30 minutes) + Metro Line 3 (Tangkeng Station, Exit C) $\rightarrow$ M380 Bus (Tangkeng Metro Station $\rightarrow$ Meisha Street Office Station).
        *   Metro Line 3 $\rightarrow$ Metro Line 5 $\rightarrow$ Metro Line 8 $\rightarrow$ M196 Bus (Yantian Gangxi Metro Station $\rightarrow$ Zhongxing Development Station).
4.  **Afternoon:** Hiking at DaMeiSha Seaside Park $\rightarrow$ Yantian Seafood Street (2 hours).
5.  **~18:00:** Dinner at Yantian Seafood Street.
    *   **Dinner Suggestions:** Dong Teng Seafood Restaurant.
6.  **Return:** Yantian Seafood Street $\rightarrow$ Luohu Station (1 hour 24 minutes) via M205 Bus.
