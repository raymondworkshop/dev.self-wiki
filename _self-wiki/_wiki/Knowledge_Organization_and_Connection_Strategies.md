**Core Insight:** The intellectual integrity of any conclusion rests upon two pillars: the clarity of its foundational concepts and the depth of its empirical tracing. 

**Deficiency Identified:** 
1. **Conceptual Vagueness:** Concepts lack rigorous, agreed-upon boundaries, allowing for slippage during application. 
2. **Detail Atrophy:** Insufficient tracking of granular data prevents the conclusion from being empirically grounded or logically sound.

**Consequence:** The resulting output is either unsupported narrative (imagination) or functionally useless due to its inability to be tested or refuted (unfalsifiability).
