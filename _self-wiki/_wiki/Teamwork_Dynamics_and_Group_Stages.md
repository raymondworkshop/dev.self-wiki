## Psychological Safety and Group Development

**Core Concept:** Psychological safety is the necessary precondition for high-functioning collaborative units.

**Group Lifecycle Model (Tuckman Adaptation):**
1. **Forming/Storming:** Initial stages characterized by conflict, role negotiation, and testing boundaries. This phase requires managed tension.
2. **Norming:** Establishing shared processes, norms, and cohesion.
3. **Performing:** Achieving high-level synergy and task execution.
