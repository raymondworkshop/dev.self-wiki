> Family relationships are founded on mutual support and shared joy. Furthermore, the pursuit of a career should be viewed as a tangible expression of one's capabilities, extending beyond mere financial gain.

## Evolution
- Initial thoughts on family dynamics are being integrated with the view of work as a means of demonstrating competence.

## Content
### Family Dynamics
Family relationships are characterized by mutual support and the sharing of happiness. (Source: [[self-wiki/raw/origin-apple-notes/families.md]])

### Professional Endeavor
The pursuit of a career is a way to manifest one's abilities. It is important to recognize that the effort invested in one's work is not solely transactional; it is also a demonstration of self-worth and capability. (Source: [[self-wiki/raw/origin-apple-notes/families.md]])
