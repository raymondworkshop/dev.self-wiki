> When seeking professional advice or building a collaborative relationship, the focus must be on establishing shared goals. This involves seeking counsel, building partnership, and utilizing outcome feedback to facilitate mutual growth.

## Evolution
- Initial concept derived from the need to structure interactions when offering or receiving guidance.

## Content
### 寻求专业建议
- 这是一个主动获取知识和方向的阶段，需要清晰地界定问题域。

### 建立合作关系
- 合作的基础是双方都愿意投入资源和精力，共同指向一个目标。

### 通过结果反馈
- 真正的合作不是单向的指导，而是通过对结果的共同评估和反馈循环，实现双方的成长。
