## Content
### The Art of Tactful Expression
Effective communication requires more than just conveying information; it demands emotional intelligence to navigate the recipient's frame of reference. The concept of "being tactful" implies a conscious effort to soften bluntness, ensuring that the message is received with consideration rather than defensiveness.

### Identified Gaps
When reviewing the current state of expression, two key deficiencies were noted:
1. **Lack of Warmth (缺乏溫度)**: The delivery felt too clinical or purely logical, missing the human element necessary for deep connection.
2. **Lack of [Missing Information] (缺乏)**: A specific element needed for completeness was identified but remains undefined in the current raw notes.

To improve, the focus must shift from *what* is said to *how* it is received.
