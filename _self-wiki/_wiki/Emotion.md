> This framework uses three critical questions to guide self-inquiry into the nature of emotion and personal growth. It prompts a comparison between internal self-view and external perception, clarifies core values, and demands actionable commitments for future development.

## Evolution
- Initial insights regarding the need for structured self-reflection on emotional states, moving beyond mere feeling to actionable understanding.

## Content
The journey through emotion requires answering three fundamental questions:
1. What are the differences between how you see yourself and how others see you?
2. What matters to you?
3. What changes will you make to achieve your goals?
