### Muscle Group Specifics
*   **Triceps (肱三头肌)**: Overhead Extension (長頭)
    *   Execution: Maintain standing posture, feet shoulder-width apart. Hold dumbbells, raise arms overhead. Bend elbows, keeping shoulders, elbows, and wrists in a straight line, slowly lower the dumbbells behind the head. Return to the starting position and repeat.
*   **Chest (胸肌)**: Flat Bench Dumbbell Press (平凳啞鈴卧推)
    *   Execution: Lie on a flat bench, feet flat on the floor, maintaining stability. Hold one dumbbell in each hand, palms facing forward at chest level. Inhale, contract core muscles, stabilize the body. Simultaneously press the dumbbells upward until the arms are almost straight, but do not lock the elbows. Exhale, slowly lower the dumbbells back to the starting position, maintaining control and preventing them from dropping.
