> This page contains a collection of Cantonese phrases and their literal translations, providing a linguistic snapshot. The phrases relate to concepts of being oneself, fullness, and waiting.

## Content
**自己** (zi6 gei2) - 确 (kok3) 白 (baak6) 低 (dai2) 青 (cing1) 数 (sou3) - 走数 = 吃霸王餐 (zhai baak6 wong1 caan)
**焕** (wun6) - 饿 (ngo6) 饱 (baau2) 排队 (paai4 deoi6) 味 (wei6) - 做 (zou6) 煮 (zyu2)
**薯条** (su4 tiu4) - 馒头 (maan4 tou2)
**位** (wai2) - 就 (zau6) 留 (lau2) 阵 (zan6) 等 (dang2) - 要 (jiu3) 走 (zou2) 再 (zoi3) 杯 (bui1) 现金 (yin6 gam1)
