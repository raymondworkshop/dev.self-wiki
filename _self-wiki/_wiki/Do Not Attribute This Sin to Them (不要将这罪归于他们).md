> This note appears to be a directive regarding interpersonal relationships, specifically concerning family matters. It suggests a need for forbearance and non-attribution of fault in close relationships.



### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/origin-apple-notes/对家人要好些.md]]
The core message is to exercise kindness and understanding towards family members. This suggests that in close bonds, minor frictions should not escalate into assigning blame or holding grudges. This aligns with the broader theme of managing relational dynamics under stress.
