*   **课 (fo3)**: 读 (Read/Study)
*   **公司 (gung1 si1)**: 客户 haak3 wu6 (Client)
*   **联络 (lyun4 lok3)**: 入 rup6 (Enter/Go into)
*   **厕所 (ci3 so2)**: 差 caa1 (Bad/Poor)
*   **剧 (kek6)**: 之 (Of/Possessive particle)
*   **痛 (tung3)**: 伤 soeng1 (Injury/Pain)
*   **节 (zit3)**: 秋cau1 (Autumn)
