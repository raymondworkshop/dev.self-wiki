> This framework outlines a multi-layered approach to problem analysis, requiring the articulation of inferential steps and maintaining breadth in potential solutions. It mandates tracing the context of an issue from its smallest manifestation to its broadest systemic root.

## Evolution
- Initial concepts regarding problem decomposition and solution generation are being formalized into a structured cognitive model.

## Content
### 推论阶梯 (Inference Ladder)
1. **解释推论阶梯 (Explain the Inference Ladder)**: It is crucial to articulate the chain of reasoning used to reach a conclusion.
2. **问题所属层次 (Level of Problem)**: The analysis must begin by locating the problem within its proper context: `event -> pattern -> system/organization -> vision`.
3. **多选项原则 (Multiple Options Principle)**: When a solution is found, it must be accompanied by consideration of alternative paths or options.
