> Fairness is fundamentally a manifestation of power, defined and upheld by those who possess it. This definition, however, is inherently unstable and can be overthrown by a superior power structure.

## Evolution
- Initial conceptualization of power dynamics applied to the concept of "fairness."

## Content
The concept presented suggests that the notion of "fairness" is not an objective, universal constant, but rather a tool or construct wielded by the dominant group. The powerful establish the rules of what is considered fair, and this establishment is contingent upon their continued dominance. If a stronger power emerges, they possess the authority to redefine or overturn the previous standard of fairness.
