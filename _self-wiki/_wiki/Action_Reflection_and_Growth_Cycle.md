> This cycle outlines that true growth is not linear but cyclical, requiring the integration of planning, execution, critical reflection, and subsequent revision. It moves beyond simple execution by demanding analysis of outcomes to inform the next strategic action.

## Evolution
- Initial concepts regarding learning were fragmented; this page synthesizes them into a cohesive, actionable loop.

## Content
### The Growth Mindset Praxis
Praxis is defined as the progression achieved through the combination of **reflection and action**. This is an active, dynamic process:

1.  **Plan and Action**: Implementing a strategy or taking a course of action.
2.  **Reflection**: Critically analyzing the results of the action taken.
3.  **Improve**: Revising and altering conceptions and future plans based on the reflection.
4.  **Plan and Action Again**: Implementing the revised plan in subsequent actions.

### Courage vs. Confidence
*   **Courage**: The internal fortitude required to both stand up and speak, but equally, to sit down and listen.
*   **Confidence**: This is an external manifestation, whereas courage originates internally.
