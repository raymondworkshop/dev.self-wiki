> This page synthesizes notes on maintaining high energy levels through lifestyle factors, including sleep, exercise, and diet. It emphasizes the importance of consistent routines to sustain optimal cognitive and physical function.

## Evolution
- Initial notes focused on the feeling of constant tiredness and brainstorming solutions related to activity and intake.

## Content
### Energy Maintenance Factors
*   **Exercise**: Consider increasing physical activity.
*   **Sleep**: Aim for 8-9 hours of sleep per night.
*   **Diet**: Maintain proper nutrition.
*   **Caffeine Intake**: Coffee in the morning is noted, but intake should cease after lunch or 12 PM to avoid disrupting sleep patterns (misuse noted: 5-7 hours).

### Self-Approval vs. External Validation
*   **Self-Approval**: True validation must come from within, not from external social approval.
*   **Action as Proof**: Let your actions speak for themselves; let your execution silence doubters and detractors.
