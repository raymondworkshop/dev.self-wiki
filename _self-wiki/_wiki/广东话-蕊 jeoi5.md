> This entry maps Cantonese phonetic sounds to corresponding Mandarin/English concepts, organizing them into domains like career, family, and emotional states. It serves as a foundational glossary for integrating Cantonese cultural context into the knowledge base.

## Evolution
- Initial mapping of sounds and concepts.

## Content
**Domain Mapping:**
- **事业 (si6 jip6)**: Career/Work
- **亲情 (can1 cing4)**: Family/Kinship
- **高 (gou1)**: High/High standard (context needed)
- **抢 (coeng2)**: To snatch/To compete
- **床铺 (cong4 pou3)**: Bed/Sleeping arrangement

**Emotional/Situational States:**
- **怨 (yun3)**: Resentment/Grievance
- **能干 (nang4 gon3)**: Capable/Competent
- **温暖 (wan1 nyun5)**: Warmth/Heartfelt
- **故事 (gu3 si6)**: Story/Narrative
- **烦 (faan4)**: Annoyance/Trouble
- **或者 (waak6)**: Or/Perhaps
