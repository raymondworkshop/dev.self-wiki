### Developing a BATNA (Best Alternative To a Negotiated Agreement)
A robust understanding of one's BATNA is crucial as it forms the baseline for any negotiation. Without a clearly defined and executable alternative, one enters negotiations from a position of weakness, making compromise less powerful.
