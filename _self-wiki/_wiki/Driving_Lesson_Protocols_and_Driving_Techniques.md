## Content

### Vehicle Controls & Pre-Drive Checks
- **Starting/Stopping:**
    - **Starting:** Adjust mirrors (rear view parallel to rear window; side mirrors showing 1/4 of the body/door handle position). Right foot presses brake pedal. When in P, pressing the brake pedal engages a safety lock, preventing accidental shifting into other gears.
    - **Parking:** Apply handbrake, shift to N (Neutral), then apply brake.
    - **Starting from Stop:** Apply brake, then shift to D (Drive) and release the handbrake.
- **Driving States:**
    - **P/R/N/D:** P (Park - stationary), R (Reverse), N (Neutral - short stop), D (Drive - moving forward).
    - **Braking/Accelerating:** When starting from a stop, the right foot presses the brake pedal. When moving, the foot controls the pedals.

### Maneuvers & Situational Awareness
- **Lane Changing/Overtaking:**
    - **Procedure:** Check mirrors (all three), signal, check blind spot, then execute the change.
    - **Advanced:** When overtaking, maintain awareness of the vehicle ahead (watching 2-3 cars ahead) and monitor adjacent lane movements.
- **Turning:**
    - **90-degree Left Turn:** Requires turning the steering wheel one full rotation.
    - **General Turn:** Before turning, mentally commit to the intended lane. For a left turn, you might move into the left lane first.
    - **Right Turn:** Drive forward until reaching a safe island/turning point, then begin turning, ensuring you are not obstructing the dividing line.
    - **Left Turn:** Begin turning after crossing the left white line, encompassing the curve without obstructing the dividing line.
- **Blind Spot Checks:** Crucial for all maneuvers (lane change, overtaking, turning). Always check mirrors and then turn your head to confirm the blind spot.
- **Road Positioning:**
    - **Center Line:** The vehicle should ideally drive in the middle of its lane.
    - **White Lines (Solid/Broken):** A solid line on your side means you must not cross it. A broken line allows crossing if safe.
    - **Warning Lines:** Longer, shorter gaps indicate potential danger ahead; proceed with caution.
- **Specific Routes/Situations:**
    - **Roundabouts (回旋处):** Entering means yielding to traffic already in the roundabout. Exiting requires signaling and checking for traffic approaching from the rear/left.
    - **S-Turn Parking:** Requires precise execution of reverse movements, using mirrors and indicators, ensuring the vehicle remains within the white lines.
