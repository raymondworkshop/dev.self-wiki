> This entry reflects on the ideal qualities of a companion or partner, suggesting a balance between self-sufficiency and shared depth. The ideal presence is characterized by independence, generosity, and a cultured demeanor.

## Evolution
- Initial thoughts regarding the qualities desired in a significant relationship partner.

## Content
The ideal companion possesses the following qualities:
1. **Independence (独立)**: Possessing a strong sense of self and autonomy.
2. **Generosity (大方)**: Displaying openness and magnanimity in interactions.
3. **Refinement/Culture (有气质, 知书达理)**: Possessing good taste, cultural knowledge, and intellectual depth.

These traits suggest a partnership built on two whole individuals meeting in mutual respect, rather than dependence.
