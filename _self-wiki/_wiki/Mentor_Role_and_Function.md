> A mentor serves as a multifaceted guide, assisting in skill development through constructive feedback, providing directional advice on career or life paths, and actively advocating for the mentee's advancement. This role moves beyond simple advice to active partnership in growth.

## Evolution
- Initial thoughts on mentorship were fragmented; this entry synthesizes the core functions into a structured model.

## Content
### Core Functions of Mentorship

1. **Skill Development and Feedback:** To help develop skills and abilities by providing constructive feedback aimed at improvement.
2. **Guidance and Direction:** To offer counsel regarding career paths, academic pursuits, or life choices.
3. **Advocacy and Sponsorship:** To act as a guide who actively champions the mentee's cause, securing opportunities such as jobs or promotions.
