> True education involves sustained immersion in one's passions, leading to a unique taste and vision. Innovation, in this context, is the ability to establish a standard that others willingly follow. This pursuit requires consistent, dedicated time investment in self-discovery.

## Evolution
- Initial thoughts focused on the importance of following one's heart and interests. This has evolved into a framework where sustained intellectual labor is the mechanism by which personal passion translates into influential standards.

## Content
### The Pursuit of Taste and Depth
The journey begins with the individual's commitment to their own interests.
> <b><u>follow your heart, follow your passion, follow your interests</u></b>
> <b><u>每天化四五个小时思考自己喜欢的东西</u></b>

This dedicated time investment allows for the development of a broad knowledge base, which cultivates a refined "taste."
> 广泛的知识，<b><u>使自己有好的taste</u></b>

### The Mechanism of Influence (Innovation)
The ultimate goal is not just personal satisfaction but the capacity to guide others.
> 希望自己喜欢的东西，别人也喜欢， 也会跟过来
> 所谓创新，就是<b><u>设置标准，让别人跟</u></b>

This suggests that genuine innovation stems from the successful alignment of deep personal conviction with external resonance.
