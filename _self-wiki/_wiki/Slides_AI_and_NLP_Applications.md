> This document synthesizes various technical presentations spanning machine learning, natural language processing (NLP), and artificial intelligence (AI) from different years. The content ranges from cognitive behavioral therapy applications to advanced neural network architectures and legal text processing.

## Evolution
- Initial entries were highly technical, focusing on specific algorithms (e.g., word embeddings, CNNs, RNNs) and their application in academic settings.
- Later entries show a shift towards applying these technologies to real-world problems like mental health support and legal text analysis.

## Content
### 2025-9-16 Mental Health for Every Mind
- **AI-powered cognitive behavioral therapist:** Introduction to a personal AI designed to function as a CBT therapist. (Source: [[self-wiki/raw/diary/slides.md]])

### 2023-04-08 Factoid Question Answering (QA)
- **Question Answering (QA):** Summary of recent progress on QA tasks, where an answer is generated given a specific question. (Source: [[self-wiki/raw/diary/slides.md]])

### 2022-06-18 NLP in Legal Domain
- **NLP Applications:** Exploration of NLP applied to legal texts, including judgments, scholarly articles, arguments, and pleadings, noting that commercial texts are not the only domain. (Source: [[self-wiki/raw/diary/slides.md]])

### 2021-08 Japan in Views of Culture
- **Cultural Study:** Presentation on Japanese culture viewed through the lens of HKU SPACE. (Source: [[self-wiki/raw/diary/slides.md]])

### 2017-2020 AI and Language Applications
- **Machine Learning Applications:** Work accepted at apiems2018 detailing projects such as:
    - Mapping Customer Needs to Product Specifications using Word Embeddings.
    - Developing algorithms using SVM and CNNs for text classification.
    - Implementing various neural network architectures (RNN, LSTM, Hierarchical Attention Network).
    - Developing a system for Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR) for L2 learners (English & Chinese). (Source: [[self-wiki/raw/diary/slides.md]])

### 2017-05 Text Simplification
- **Text Simplification:** Research presented on a customizable editor for text simplification, accepted at COLING 2016. (Source: [[self-wiki/raw/diary/slides.md]])

### 2015-11 Privacy-preserving Similar Search
- **Information Retrieval:** Discussion on Nearest Neighbour search, focusing on finding objects with similar characteristics to a query object (e.g., biometric face matching). (Source: [[self-wiki/raw/diary/slides.md]])

### 2014-10 Online Learning
- **Online Education Trends:** Overview of the shift in higher education due to MOOCs (edX, Coursera, Udacity), detailing challenges and possibilities in technology and education. (Source: [[self-wiki/raw/diary/slides.md]])

### 2013-05 P2PSIP
- **Networking Protocols:** Presentation on the Peer-to-Peer Session Initiation Protocol (P2PSIP) and Distributed Hash Tables (DHTs) for enabling real-time communication. (Source: [[self-wiki/raw/diary/slides.md]])

### 2013-04 Incremental 3D Reconstruction
- **Computer Vision Algorithm:** An essay detailing an algorithm for 3D reconstruction that incrementally updates a model by searching for correlated patches when new images are added, applicable to large-scale or real-time datasets. (Source: [[self-wiki/raw/diary/slides.md]])

### 2013-04 Finding Better Experts
- **Social Network Analysis:** A report on finding better experts in micro-blogging services, utilizing the TwitterRank algorithm to measure user influence based on topical similarity and link structure. (Source: [[self-wiki/raw/diary/slides.md]])
