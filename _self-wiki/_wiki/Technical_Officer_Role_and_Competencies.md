## Content
### Technical Officer Responsibilities (Research Project Support)
- Assist in software development and maintenance to support the Research Project.

### Applicant Requirements (Technical Officer)
- **Education/Experience**: Relevant bachelor’s degree or above in IT, Computer Science, or related field, with at least 6 years of relevant experience.
- **Programming Proficiency**: Extensive experience with at least one programming language (e.g., Java, Python, C++).
- **Technical Skills**: Relevant experience in most of the following areas:
    - Building responsive UI.
    - Programming, software development, and maintenance.
    - Tender preparation and evaluation.
    - Preferred skills (opportunity to learn provided): Innovative techniques such as cloud computing, big data analytics, and Deep Learning technologies.
    - Good experience in planning, security, project management, and documentation.
- **Soft Skills/Maturity**:
    - Mature, self-motivated, able to work independently, and willing to work on shift.
    - Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills.
    - Customer-oriented mindset with good interpersonal and communication skills.

*Note: Candidates with less experience may be considered for the post of Assistant Technical Officer.*
