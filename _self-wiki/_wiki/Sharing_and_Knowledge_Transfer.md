> The act of sharing knowledge requires careful consideration, especially given the speed of modern technological development. The mindset of "small-scale thinking" suggests that sharing might sometimes be viewed as a risk or a loss of control.

## Evolution
- Initial thoughts on sharing were rooted in a mindset that viewed knowledge as something to be hoarded or guarded, contrasting with the current reality of rapid information exchange.

## Content
### Small-Scale Thinking vs. Modern Sharing
The concept of "small-scale thinking" suggests a reluctance or inability to share knowledge freely. In the context of rapid technological advancement, this reluctance is challenged. The modern context suggests that sharing, when done correctly, can actually enhance one's own mastery over the subject matter.
