> This document compiles basic conversational scripts for greetings, weather commentary, and checking in on recent events, emphasizing the nuances of natural small talk. It serves as a practical guide for navigating initial social interactions.

## Content
### Greeting People
**Initiating Conversation:**
- "Hey, how’s it going?"
- "Hi, how are you?"
- "Hey, you alright?"

**Responding to Greetings:**
- "I’m good."
- "Yeah good."
- "Pretty good."
- "Not bad."
- "I’m alright."

**Reciprocal Inquiry:**
- "You?"
- "Yourself?"

### Discussing Weather
**Initiating Weather Talk:**
- "Lovely weather, isn’t it?"
- "Awful weather, isn’t it?"
- "Miserable"

**Responding to Weather Talk:**
- "Yes, gorgeous."
- "I know, dreadful."
- "It’s pouring."
- "There’s a bit of rain."
- "I’m absolutely/really drenched."

**Checking In:**
- "What’s new?"
- "Not much, I’ve just been working. How about you?"

**Cultural Note:**
- Small talk should avoid appearing boastful or overly performative.
