> This document synthesizes the philosophy of approaching complex problems by refusing to remain siloed in one domain. It advocates for the constant cross-pollination of ideas, treating every experience as a data point for growth.
>
> The core idea is to combine the imaginative leaps of the poet with the rigorous testing of the scientist to generate and validate novel concepts.

## Evolution
- Initial concepts regarding the need for breadth of knowledge have been formalized into actionable principles regarding the integration of different modes of thought (imagination vs. empiricism).

## Content
### Core Principles for Mastery and Innovation

1. **Treat Every Situation as a Learning Opportunity:** View all experiences, successes, and failures as valuable data points to absorb and integrate into a larger framework. (Source: [[self-wiki/raw/origin-apple-notes/research idea.md]])
2. **Seek Cross-Fertilization:** Do not restrict knowledge to a single domain. Actively seek ways to apply concepts, models, or insights from one field into another to generate novel solutions. (Source: [[self-wiki/raw/origin-apple-notes/research idea.md]])
3. **Balance Vision and Rigor:** Combine the boundless imagination of the poet (generating hypotheses) with the critical, empirical testing of the scientist (validating hypotheses). (Source: [[self-wiki/raw/origin-apple-notes/research idea.md]])
4. **Maintain Focus and Efficiency:** Minimize wasted effort on non-productive paths ("no time to waste on route") while maintaining a high standard of execution. (Source: [[self-wiki/raw/origin-apple-notes/research idea.md]])
5. **Clarity of Vision:** Maintain a clear, focused view of the external reality while allowing internal conceptual fluidity. (Source: [[self-wiki/raw/origin-apple-notes/research idea.md]])

### Operational Directives

*   **Focus on the External:** Direct attention outward to observe the reality being presented, rather than remaining trapped in internal models. (Source: [[self-wiki/raw/origin-apple-notes/research idea.md]])
*   **Pursue Depth and Breadth:** While mastering a core specialty, remain curious and engaged in adjacent fields to maintain adaptability. (Source: [[self-wiki/raw/origin-apple-notes/research idea.md]])
*   **Authenticity in Output:** Commit to articulating what is genuinely believed to be true, grounding ideas in reality. (Source: [[self-wiki/raw/origin-apple-notes/research idea.md]])
