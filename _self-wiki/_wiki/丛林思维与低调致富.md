> This page synthesizes the mindset required for self-employment and business ownership, framing risk not as a threat but as a necessary condition for growth. It contrasts the security of employment with the self-determination found in facing one's own risks.

## Evolution
- Initial concepts regarding wealth generation are being refined through the lens of modern entrepreneurial realities, emphasizing personal agency.

## Content
### Self-Employment and Intellectual Capital
Self-employed professionals derive value by selling their intellect. Business owners, in contrast, gain control over their destiny by assuming risk.

### The Nature of Risk
Risk is defined here not merely as external market volatility, but as the choice to work for a ruthless employer, which represents a surrender of control.

### Growth Through Adversity
The ability to grow stronger and wiser comes from actively confronting risk and adversity. The individual who engages with risk daily is the one who tests their courage daily and, consequently, learns to conquer fear.
