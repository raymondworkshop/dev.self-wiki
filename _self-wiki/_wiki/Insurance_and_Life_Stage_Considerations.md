It highlights the tension between maintaining professional composure and the reality of transactional relationships in modern life.

### 1. Financial & Insurance Planning
*   **Investment:** Allocation of ~500/M into HSBC Global Equity Index Fund.
*   **Insurance Needs:**
    *   Health/Medical Insurance: Planning around parental coverage (~500/Y) vs. self-coverage (@50).
    *   Product Review: Discussed various types including Critical Illness, Medical Insurance (with deductibles), Bowtie Insurance, and specific plans (e.g., 保柏环球优越自愿医保计划).
    *   Action Item: Physical examination (体检) pending.
*   **Costs:** Exam fee (220 HKD), Insurance fee (252 HKD).

### 2. Social Dynamics & Self-Positioning (Experiences)
*   **Work Environment:** Acknowledgment that professional settings are inherently transactional ('利益场'). The need to establish positive rapport ('vibes') and adapt communication style by mirroring successful counterparts ('copy 对方').
*   **Social Filter:** Observation that genuine independent thinkers and altruists are rare; the necessity to learn to filter friends and practice small talk with strangers.
*   **Self-Assessment:** The tendency to be overly accommodating ('too nice') and failing to maintain an equal footing in relationships, stemming from a background in a close-knit community ('熟人社会长大'), leading to susceptibility to others' words; non-verbal cues and 'vibe' are deemed more trustworthy.
