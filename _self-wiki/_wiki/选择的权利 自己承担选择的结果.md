> This principle asserts that the freedom to make choices inherently carries the full responsibility for the outcomes of those choices. It is a fundamental acknowledgment of agency in both personal and systemic contexts.



### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/origin-apple-notes/选择的权利, 自己承担选择的结果.md]]
**Principle: Agency and Accountability**
The right to choose is inseparable from the duty to accept the consequences of that choice. This is not merely a suggestion but a core operating parameter for self-governance.

**Connection to '如何快乐的能力' (The Ability to Be Happy):**
True happiness, as suggested by the pairing, likely arises from the successful integration of this principle—the ability to choose paths that align with one's values and then willingly accept the journey those choices dictate, including potential friction or difficulty.

**[AI Synthesis]:** This concept functions as a high-level filter for decision paralysis. If one accepts the full weight of the outcome, the paralysis caused by fear of the unknown outcome diminishes, allowing for decisive action.
