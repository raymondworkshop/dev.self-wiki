> This concept contrasts zero-sum games, where outcomes are fixed and require strategy/deception to win, with infinite games, where value can be co-created, making honesty a path to mutual gain. The shift in mindset from scarcity to abundance is crucial for sustainable success.

## Evolution
- Initial thoughts on competitive environments are being formalized into a cognitive framework for strategic interaction.

## Content
### Zero-Sum Games
In a zero-sum game, resources are finite. Winning necessitates that someone else loses, leading to environments where tactics like 'deception' or 'scheming' become necessary for victory.

### Infinite Games
In an infinite game, the possibility exists to create more resources than initially present. The standard for success shifts from merely winning to understanding *how* to generate greater value, potentially even for others, making honesty a realistic path to mutual benefit.
