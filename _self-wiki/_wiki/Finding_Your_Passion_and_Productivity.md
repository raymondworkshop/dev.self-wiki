> Those who create, possess talent, and produce are often perceived as having mental stability, clarity, and drive. The core message is the necessity of finding something you are truly passionate about and committing to learning about it obsessively.

## Evolution
- Initial thoughts focused on observing successful individuals and extrapolating the necessary internal states (stability, clarity, drive) required for high output. This has evolved into a directive: the pursuit must be driven by genuine, obsessive love for the subject matter.

## Content
The observation is that individuals who are creators, talented, and productive often appear to possess a profound inner steadiness, sharp vision, and powerful execution capability. (Source: [[self-wiki/raw/origin-apple-notes/只羡慕那些会创造、有才华、能产出的人，觉得他们心是稳的，眼是亮的，手脚是有力的。真的，你一定要去找到你热爱的东西，愿意为之疯狂学习….md]])

The actionable conclusion drawn is that one must locate a pursuit that ignites genuine passion, and then commit to learning about it to the point of obsession. This deep commitment transforms the pursuit into a life mission, echoing the feeling of having been moved by a past moment of personal realization.
