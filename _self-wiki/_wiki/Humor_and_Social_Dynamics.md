> This page synthesizes observations on humor, the complexities of modern relationships, and the nuances of social interaction, drawing from anecdotal accounts and personal reflections. It touches upon the gap between idealized connection and lived reality.

## Evolution
- Initial entries were anecdotal observations regarding dining experiences, personal relationships, and the nature of perfection vs. reality.
- The content has been integrated into a broader framework concerning cognitive comparison and social performance.

## Content

### Observations on Humor and Social Performance
*   **The Dining Anecdote (倪匡的妙法):** A story illustrating the tendency to seek value at the extremes. The anecdote about the restaurant menu (smallest font = hidden service charge) mirrors the tendency to overlook fine print in life or agreements. (Source: [[self-wiki/raw/diary/language/2020-04-23-humor.md]])
*   **The Paleontologist's Precision:** The anecdote regarding the dinosaur's age demonstrates how accumulated experience (or learned narrative) can lead to confident, yet potentially flawed, specificity. (Source: [[self-wiki/raw/diary/language/2020-04-23-humor.md]])
*   **The Nature of Imperfection:** The idea that imperfections make life more relatable ("i mean, maybe the imperfections make it more like life. cause life isn't perfect") contrasts with the pursuit of flawless execution. (Source: [[self-wiki/raw/diary/language/2020-04-23-humor.md]])

### Reflections on Love and Connection
*   **The Intensity of Feeling:** The raw emotional outpouring regarding attraction ("I really like you," "Love is passion, obsession...") highlights the high-stakes nature of deep emotional investment. This contrasts with the intellectualized approach to relationships. (Source: [[self-wiki/raw/diary/language/2020-04-23-humor.md]])
*   **The Spectrum of Affection:** The spectrum ranges from highly complimentary ("You are my favorite person here") to the vulnerability of admitting inability to sustain expectations ("I know i have no right to expect or demand anything about you"). (Source: [[self-wiki/raw/diary/language/2020-04-23-humor.md]])
*   **The Pursuit of Depth:** The philosophical musing that true living requires risking deep emotional attachment ("To make the journey and not fall deeply in love - well, you haven't lived a life at all") frames commitment as a necessary risk. (Source: [[self-wiki/raw/diary/language/2020-04-23-humor.md]])

### Communication and Politeness Dynamics
*   **The Art of Deference:** Examples illustrate the performance of politeness in social settings, from accepting compliments gracefully to navigating scheduling conflicts ("有點野 想找你, 今晚得不得閒?"). (Source: [[self-wiki/raw/diary/language/2020-04-23-humor.md]])
*   **The Weight of Presence:** The contrast between being present and being truly *seen* is implied in the various scenarios, where body language, timing, and unspoken context carry significant weight. (Source: [[self-wiki/raw/diary/language/2020-04-23-humor.md]])
