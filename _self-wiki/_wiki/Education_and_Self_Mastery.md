> Sustained, uninterrupted iteration within an open environment creates a complex network system, regardless of superficial agility. By ensuring this iterative growth remains undisturbed over an extended period, one can transcend initial goals.

## Evolution
- Initial thoughts focused on the mechanics of complex system development through sustained iteration.

## Content
在开放环境下持续的，不被打断的，不断迭代造就的复杂网络系统； 这和外表的抖机灵没关系

只要保证自己的迭代成长不被打扰打断，只要争取迭代很长的时间，就可以超越目标
