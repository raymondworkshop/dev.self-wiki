> Scaling technology businesses hinges on two critical factors: drastically reducing costs and achieving significant scale. Furthermore, external factors, such as supportive government policy, are often necessary catalysts for widespread adoption and investment.

## Evolution
- Initial thoughts on scaling technology ventures, now formalized into core principles regarding cost, scale, and policy enablement.

## Content
### Scaling Drivers
The key to scaling any technology venture lies in:
1. **Cost Reduction:** Achieving low operational costs is paramount.
2. **Scalability:** The solution must be capable of scaling to a large market base.

### Market Adoption & Investment
*   **Catalytic Projects:** Initial projects can serve as catalysts, providing necessary seed funding and attracting subsequent investors.
*   **Policy Dependence:** Without appropriate governmental policies, scaling remains severely constrained.
