> This concept establishes the foundational pillars of any valuable relationship: genuine warmth and reliability. It moves beyond mere pleasantries to define a commitment to being dependable in both action and word. This principle is critical for deep connection and transactional integrity.



### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/origin-apple-notes/友善，值得信任.md]]
## Principle: Trust and Friendliness

**Definition:** The combination of consistent kindness (友善) and proven reliability (值得信任) forms the bedrock of any meaningful connection.

**Components of Trustworthiness:**
1. **Consistency:** Actions must align with stated values over time.
2. **Vulnerability/Honesty:** Willingness to be seen authentically, which requires candor.
3. **Follow-Through:** Executing commitments, no matter how small.

**Cognitive Link:** This principle acts as a filter for potential collaborators and friends, separating transient acquaintances from genuine partners. (Source: [[origin-apple-notes/友善，值得信任.md]])
