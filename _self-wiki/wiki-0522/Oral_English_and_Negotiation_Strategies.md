## Linguistic Strategies for Oral Fluency

### Speaking Pace & Thought Generation
*   Slow down your speaking speed and select words carefully.
*   Buy time with phrases like: "That's a good question. Let me think for a moment," or "I haven't thought about it before..."

### Comprehension & Interaction
*   Practice active listening to fully grasp others' points.
*   **Practice asking questions**: "What are your views on that?" or "How about you, what do you think?"

### Register & Politeness
*   **Requests**: Use modal verbs like *can, could, may*. Example: "Can I have [X], please?"
*   **Declining**: Offer a softened refusal. Example: "That is a good idea. I would like to join in but I have another appointment today."
*   **Apologizing**: Use formal phrasing: "I sincerely apologise," "I owe you an apology," or "I take full responsibility...".

### Idiomatic & Contextual Phrases (From '76 Opening Night')
*   "You've earned it." (你应得的)
*   "It's common practice." (这是惯例)
*   "I'm scared to death." (紧张,我害怕的要命)
*   "They've stood by me through all this." (整个过程中一直支持我)
*   "If you really mean it, I'll bring some..."
