> This entry documents the principle of maintaining composure under extreme duress, mirroring Amundsen's successful navigation of survival scenarios. The core insight is the application of rigorous training and rationality to sustain life when resources are depleted.

### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/origin-apple-notes/writing.md]]
Amundsen maintained his rationality during the ordeal and meticulously documented all events. He utilized his acquired technical knowledge, particularly concerning emergency medical care, provisions, and clothing, to aid the expedition's survival. (Source: [[origin-apple-notes/writing.md]])

### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/origin-apple-notes/insight  important self.md]]
**Core Principle: Pursuit vs. Attainment**
- The pursuit of meaning is inherently spiritual; worldly success is transient and easily accommodated.
- Material success and fame are secondary byproducts, not the primary goal, which fosters a state of 'calmness' (淡定).

**Cognitive Observation: Cultural Mindset**
- Observed tendency toward closed-mindedness in certain cultural contexts mirrors an 'agricultural mindset.'
- Contrast this with the observed management style characterized by 'militarization.'

**Epistemological Filter**
- A statement holds validity if it is logically sound, regardless of the speaker's background.
- Interaction with those who disregard objective facts is cognitively draining, as they fail to engage with reality itself.
