> This entry documents a period of self-reflection regarding personal productivity and lifestyle habits between late 2017. Key themes include the need for stricter adherence to routines (sleep, exercise, coding practice) and a critical assessment of past social engagements and the pace of personal development.



### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/diary/diary/2017-12-07-daily-summary.md]]
**2017-12-07:** Acknowledged late rising and the need for immediate behavioral correction upon waking.

**2017-12-11:** Established the principle against staying up late and the necessity of practicing English oral skills.

**2017-12-26:** Positive reinforcement from social events (Goj dinner, sincerity activity) contrasted with the need to establish firm routines: early sleep, early rise, and dedicated practice blocks (4 hours coding, 1 hour English).

**2017-01-08:** Experienced a significant lapse in routine (waking at 14:00) and identified a core resistance to sustained mental effort.

**2017-01-09:** Achieved a minor procedural victory by waking at 9:30.

**2017-01-10:** Further noted late rising and the tendency toward passive consumption (browsing pages). A critical meta-reflection on social activities revealed:
*   Initial over-investment in social efforts.
*   A perceived cultural/generational gap (specifically regarding mainland vs. HK context) leading to caution in forming bonds.
*   The wisdom of setting boundaries: understanding the meaning of 'leading' and the necessity of saying 'no' while remaining generally polite.

**## Synthesis & Cognitive Observation**
This period demonstrates a pattern of high aspiration followed by execution failure, necessitating a conscious recalibration of social expectations and personal discipline to match the required level of effort.
