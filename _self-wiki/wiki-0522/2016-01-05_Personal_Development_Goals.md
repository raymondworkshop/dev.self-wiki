## 2016-01-05 Personal Development Goals

**Goals Established:**

*   **Language:**
    *   Achieve fluency in English (continue classes).
    *   Basic Cantonese conversation (addressing previous shortcomings).

*   **Physical Conditioning:**
    *   Gain 15 jin (approx. 7.5 kg).
    *   Establish regular sleep schedule.
    *   Running at least three times per week.
    *   Learn swimming.
    *   Organize weekly badminton sessions.
    *   Participate in group sports when opportunities arise.

*   **Intellectual Pursuits & Hobbies:**
    *   Basic sketching (focus on portraiture).
    *   Read one book every two months outside of professional texts.

*   **Social & Experiential:**
    *   Participate in or organize a meaningful activity.
    *   Make new friends.
    *   Travel to Northern Europe.

*   **Career & Professional:**
    *   Enhance system-level competencies.
    *   Identify personal areas of focus/strength.
