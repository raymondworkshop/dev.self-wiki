> Genuine connection requires the active consideration of the friend's perspective and emotional state. This foundation must be upheld by consistent compassion and unwavering commitment to truthfulness.



### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/origin-apple-notes/learning friendships.md]]
**Principles of Friendship (Synthesis of Values)**

1. **Thoughtfulness (为朋友着想):** The relationship requires proactive consideration of the other party's needs and viewpoint, moving beyond mere presence.
2. **Compassion:** An active empathy toward the friend's experiences and emotional landscape.
3. **Truthfulness:** The maintenance of trust is contingent upon fidelity to reality in communication.
