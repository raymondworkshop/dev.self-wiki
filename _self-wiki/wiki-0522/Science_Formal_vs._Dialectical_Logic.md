> This entry contrasts two modes of reasoning: Formal Logic (binary opposition, 'either/or') and Dialectical Logic (holism, interconnectedness, 'both/and'). It posits that while Western thought leans toward the former, Chinese thought traditionally favors the latter, suggesting a cultural difference in cognitive approach.

### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/origin-apple-notes/science 4.md]]
科学的理解存在两种视角：一是将其视为绝对正确的真理；二是将其视为一个不断探索未知、修正假设的实践过程。后一种观点更贴合实践的现实。

专家级的认知调整能力体现在：他们并非不会犯错，而是具备根据专业知识体系，持续迭代和修正认知的能力，从而使推论尽可能接近客观事实。
