## Principle: Unconditional Pursuit

The principle observed from Ho Tsun-Sun is the absolute refusal to decline a challenge, regardless of perceived difficulty. This mindset suggests that the initial commitment level must match the scale of the undertaking to ensure successful execution and drive.

**Core Tenet:** To say 'no' to a challenge is to preemptively accept failure. The pursuit must be absolute from the outset.
