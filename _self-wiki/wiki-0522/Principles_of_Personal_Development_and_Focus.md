### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/origin-apple-notes/精力： 作息规律，多锻炼.md]]
**Core Principles for Sustained Energy and Growth:**
1. **Learning from Surroundings:** Observe and emulate successful individuals in your environment.
2. **Focus & Efficiency:** Minimize engagement in low-value activities; prioritize self-enhancement.
3. **Linguistic Mastery:** Dedicate significant time to language acquisition, specifically English and Cantonese.
4. **Continuous Reading:** Maintain a habit of reading at least one book per month.

**Problematic Tendencies Identified:**
1. **Over-Attunement to External Validation:** Over-focusing on how one's words are received or whether they meet an external standard, rather than trusting the inherent value of the utterance.

**Operational Goal:** The immediate priority in social settings must be to cultivate an atmosphere of goodwill, friendliness, and mutual non-aggression, ensuring the interaction remains low-stakes and mutually beneficial in the short term.

### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/origin-apple-notes/love  self.md]]
**The Primacy of Passion and Conviction**
A man's character is forged by deep convictions and the pursuit of a singular passion. This passion is not merely a hobby; it is the life-long pursuit that provides the necessary drive, confidence, and joy that transforms one's existence. It demands total investment of time and desire.

**The Attributes of Driven Action**
This pursuit yields essential traits: ambition, decisiveness, backbone, kindness, stability, and confidence. Success requires setting goals, striving to excel in endeavors, and possessing the conviction to lead and solve problems rather than assigning blame.

**Attitude Towards Failure and Self**
Failure must be reframed as a temporary obstacle on the path to inevitable success. Crucially, one must never feel the need to constantly prove oneself to external parties; advancement in career, social life, and relationships flows from internal conviction.

**The Role of Partnership**
The role of a partner is to enhance one's life, not to constitute the entirety of it. The primary focus remains on the individual's internal drive and goals.

**Cognitive Note on Relationships**
The pursuit of self-mastery must precede and sustain the pursuit of connection. The individual's internal architecture must be sound before seeking external validation or completion.
