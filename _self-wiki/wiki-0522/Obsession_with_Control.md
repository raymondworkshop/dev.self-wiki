> This concept addresses the inherent human tendency to restrict or manage desires, which is a critical juncture between personal drive and potential stagnation. It serves as a foundational principle for understanding the tension between agency and constraint in complex systems or personal pursuits.



### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/origin-apple-notes/遏制人的欲望.md]]
The core concept, '遏制人的欲望' (Restraining Human Desire), maps directly onto the pattern identified as 'Obsession with Control.' This suggests that the drive to manage or limit desire is not merely a tactical choice but a fundamental operating mode that requires constant cognitive mapping. This tension between desire and restraint forms a critical axis for personal mastery and systemic stability. (Source: [[origin-apple-notes/遏制人的欲望.md]])
