> In a high-context cultural setting like China, trust must be strategically deployed. Relationships operate on distinct modes: transactional with strangers, mixed with acquaintances, and deeply emotional with close kin.

### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/origin-apple-notes/china  softskills.md]]
**中国人际关系模式 (Chinese Interpersonal Relationship Modes)**
* **陌生人 (Strangers):** 工具理性 (Instrumental Rationality) $\rightarrow$ 交易型关系 (Transactional Relationship). Governed by the principle of fairness: '合则来，不合则去' (Come if it suits, leave if it doesn't).
* **熟人 (Acquaintances):** 混合性关系 (Mixed Relationship). Governed by the principle of sentiment: '有恩报恩，有仇报仇' (Repay kindness, avenge injury).
* **亲友 (Close Kin/Friends):** 情感关系 (Emotional Relationship). Governed by the principle of need: '各尽所能，各取所需' (Each gives their best, each takes what is needed).
**信任的部署 (Deployment of Trust):**
* 对一两位导师，需无条件信任 (Absolute trust is reserved for one or two mentors).
* 对多数人，需采取'对事不对人'的态度 (Focus on the task, not the person).
(Source: [[origin-apple-notes/china softskills.md]])

### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/origin-apple-notes/obser.md]]
**Core Observations (Level 0 Distillation):**
*   Integration requires valuing friends who offer genuine help and adhering to universal rules, not those who gain entry through promises and then deceive.
*   Once familiar with the rules, the goal is not to adapt to them, but to actively seek loopholes or exploit vulnerabilities.
*   The underlying driver is extreme self-interest based on transactional gain.

**Insight (Level 1 Synthesis):**
In the eyes of those involved, one ignites the flame of altruism, truth, and dignity within the public sphere.

### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/origin-apple-notes/相互信任，相互给予.md]]
**Principle: Reciprocal Foundation**
Relationships, whether personal or professional, are built upon a dual commitment: mutual trust and mutual contribution. This is not merely about presence, but about active, valuable exchange.

**Cognitive Mapping:** The raw input suggests that the concept of 'giving' must be balanced against the expectation of 'receiving' to prevent transactional imbalance or resentment. This mirrors the tension discussed in 'Contractual Obligations and Self-Perception: The Tension of Value Exchange'.

**Actionable Insight:** To maintain high-fidelity relationships, the focus must remain on the quality and sincerity of the exchange, rather than merely the quantity of effort expended.
