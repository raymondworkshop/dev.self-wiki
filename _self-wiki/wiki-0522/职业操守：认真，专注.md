## Principle: Professional Conduct Protocol

The commitment to diligence and focus is not merely a task completion metric; it is the active demonstration of respect for the endeavor, the collaborators, and the self. It requires sustained mental presence and the refusal to allow sloppiness to enter the execution phase.

**Core Tenets of Diligence (认真):**
1. **Thoroughness in Execution:** Completing tasks to the highest standard, leaving no critical detail unaddressed. (Source: [[origin-apple-notes/职业操守：认真，专注.md]])
2. **Ownership of Output:** Accepting full responsibility for the quality of the delivered work, including its potential downstream effects.

**Core Tenets of Focus (专注):**
1. **Deep Work State:** The ability to enter and maintain a state of uninterrupted concentration necessary for complex problem-solving. (Source: [[origin-apple-notes/职业操守：认真，专注.md]])
2. **Filtering Noise:** Actively managing distractions (internal and external) to preserve cognitive resources for the task at hand.

## Application Vectors
- **Project Execution:** Applying rigorous focus to move from conceptualization to flawless delivery.
- **Self-Audit:** Applying diligence to self-reflection, ensuring that self-critique is thorough and unbiased.
