> The core sentiment suggests that achieving comfort comes from embracing authenticity and simplicity in one's being. This aligns with the need to reduce cognitive load and unnecessary complexity in execution and self-presentation.

### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/origin-apple-notes/slef.md]]
The principle articulated is that genuine comfort is found in being true to oneself and maintaining simplicity in approach. This contrasts with the tendency toward overcomplication, which breeds friction and inefficiency. (Source: [[origin-apple-notes/slef.md]])

### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/origin-apple-notes/要精.md]]
**Principle: Mental Resilience and Simplicity**
The directive '不要怕受影响' (Do not fear being influenced) suggests a proactive stance against undue external pressure, requiring the maintenance of an internal, uncompromised core.
**Actionable Insight:** The pursuit of '要精' (Essence) necessitates filtering noise and maintaining a high signal-to-noise ratio in all cognitive operations.

**Traceability:** [[origin-apple-notes/要精.md]]

### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/origin-apple-notes/活得轻松点，不要担心自己控制不了的事情.md]]
**Principle:** Maintain a relaxed state of mind and do not allow anxiety regarding uncontrollable external factors to take root.

**Distillation:** The practice of 'living lightly' involves recognizing the boundaries of personal agency. Focus energy only on variables within your sphere of influence, thereby achieving mental tranquility.

**Traceability:** (Source: [[origin-apple-notes/活得轻松点，不要担心自己控制不了的事情.md]])
