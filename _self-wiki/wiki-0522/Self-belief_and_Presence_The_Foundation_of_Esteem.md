> True eloquence stems from the ability to sustain one's own conviction; when one firmly believes in their own worth and capabilities, they naturally command respect and earn admiration. This self-sustained belief allows one to remain unmoved by external criticism and maintain internal fortitude.



### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/origin-apple-notes/education.md]]
**Core Tenet: Self-Sustained Conviction**
The ability to speak well (口才好) is fundamentally rooted in the capacity to sustain one's own belief. When one possesses sufficient capability and unwavering belief in oneself, they can weather external criticism and achieve the respect of others.

**Manifestation of Belief:**
*   **Self-Affirmation:** One must be willing to champion their own views and possess extreme self-trust.
*   **Unconditional Conviction:** The pursuit requires maintaining belief regardless of whether others concur.
*   **Source of Talent:** One's innate talent (天赋) is often found through intuition.

**Cognitive Application:**
*   **Deep Thinking:** Logic and deep thought processes are valuable for elevating the quality of life, though their immediate practical application may be limited.
*   **Training:** The process serves to train virtues such as perseverance (毅力), intelligence (IQ), and patience (耐心).
