> Maintaining consistent and proactive communication with leadership is crucial for professional alignment. This involves not only providing updates but also actively soliciting feedback to ensure mutual understanding and goal congruence.



### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/origin-apple-notes/与领导保持规律性沟通，定期积极主动寻求反馈。.md]]
The core principle is the necessity of establishing a predictable cadence of interaction with leadership. This cadence must be characterized by proactive information exchange, moving beyond mere task completion to include seeking critical input and validation on strategic direction. This ensures that individual efforts remain synchronized with organizational objectives. (Source: [[origin-apple-notes/与领导保持规律性沟通，定期积极主动寻求反馈。.md]])
