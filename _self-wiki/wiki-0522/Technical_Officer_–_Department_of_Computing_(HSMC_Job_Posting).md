## Technical Officer – Department of Computing (Ref: TO (RGC/IDS14/16) 2018-03-23)

**Responsibilities:**
* Assist in software development and maintenance in support of the Research Project.

**Applicants Should:**
* Possess a relevant bachelor’s degree or above in IT, Computer Science or relevant discipline with at least 6 years of relevant experience;
* Have extensive experience with at least one programming language (e.g., Java, Python, C++);
* Relevant experience in:
    * Building responsive UI;
    * Programming, software development and maintenance;
    * Tender preparation and evaluation;
    * Preferred skills: cloud computing, big data analytics, Deep Learning technologies (opportunity provided to learn);
* Good experience in planning, security, project management and documentation;
* Mature, self-motivated, able to work independently and willing to work on shift;
* Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills;
* Customer-oriented mindset with good interpersonal and communication skills.

*Note: Candidates with less experience will be considered for the post of Assistant Technical Officer.*
