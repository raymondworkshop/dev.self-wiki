> This summary reflects on the overall performance of the past month, noting areas of improvement across lifestyle habits, professional skills, and interpersonal conduct. Key areas identified for development include establishing a consistent routine, enhancing technical communication skills, and deepening language proficiency.



### Distillation (2026-05-21) - source: [[../raw/diary/diary/2015-07-06-weekly-summary.md]]
**Lifestyle & Habits:**
- **Routine:** Need for more consistent sleep schedule and fixed wake-up times (aiming for earlier sleep, waking before 8:30 AM).
- **Exercise:** Goal setting for 1 hour of badminton per week and 4 days of running per week.
- **Reading:** Need to increase reading speed and efficiency.
- **Language:** English requires more practice (4 TED talks/week, aiming to summarize); Cantonese goal is to achieve proficiency in the latter half of the year.
- **Interpersonal Skills:** Specific focus on improving communication with the boss.
- **Mindset:** Valuing and cherishing time.

**Professional Insights (Synthesized from External Sources):**
- **Company Fit:** Seek organizations that value both your technical execution ability AND your unique product insights, rather than those that dictate all aspects.
- **Self-Promotion:** The importance of proactively signaling major milestones or new learnings to relevant parties.
- **Key Relationships:** Recognizing that success hinges on the ability to interact effectively with others, where trust is paramount.
- **Proactivity:** The need to clearly define expectations for a new role and to operate with the mindset of ownership over one's own creative output.
- **Presence:** The necessity of being actively present in meetings and ensuring attendance only in sessions requiring participation.
