## Product Managerial Philosophy

*   **Source of Insight**: The most valuable insights leading to new products arise from engaging with hard technical problems.

*   **Development Protocol**: Adopt a 'Use Case First, Protocol Second' approach. Customers are concerned with the end-user experience, not the underlying technology; therefore, the technology architecture must serve the specific customer problem.

*   **User Focus**: Maintain a relentless focus on the users. Pursue ambitious 'moonshot' ideas, and actively engage with users to uncover the gaps in the current landscape.

*   **Mindset**: Maintain a confident, 'builder' mentality. Acknowledge potential challenges inherent in novel systems, but maintain the conviction that these hurdles can be overcome. The power comes from seeing one's creation function successfully.
