> Trust forms the bedrock of any meaningful, high-fidelity relationship, requiring consistent integrity from all parties involved. Honesty is the active commitment to this shared reality, minimizing cognitive dissonance between words and action.

### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/origin-apple-notes/信任+诚实.md]]
**Principle: Foundational Integrity**
Trust is not merely the absence of betrayal; it is the active, shared belief in another entity's commitment to truthfulness and reliability, even when that truth is difficult.
Honesty, in this context, transcends simple truth-telling; it encompasses transparency regarding one's own limitations, biases, and intentions (Self-Honesty).

**Cognitive Mapping:**
*   **Trust (The Leap):** The initial, high-risk acceptance of another's good faith.
*   **Honesty (The Maintenance):** The continuous, low-friction alignment between internal state and external expression.

**[AI Synthesis]:** The failure in either Trust or Honesty introduces entropy into the relationship system, forcing constant, high-energy verification loops rather than allowing for effortless flow.
