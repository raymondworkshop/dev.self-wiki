> Sustained effort requires acknowledgment of its inherent value and the need for rigorous application. Furthermore, maintaining professional boundaries is crucial when engaging with individuals whose perspectives do not merit sustained debate.



### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/origin-apple-notes/努力工作.md]]
**Core Tenets of Diligence:**
* Achievement is not effortless; it demands sustained, high-quality input. (Source: [[origin-apple-notes/努力工作.md]])
* Prudence dictates avoiding protracted arguments with those unwilling or unable to engage constructively. (Source: [[origin-apple-notes/努力工作.md]])

**Synthesis Note:** This reinforces the principle that high-value output requires high-value input, linking to the broader concept of 'The Pursuit of Meaningful Existence'.
