> True independence is achieved when one can manage oneself and contribute meaningfully to the world beyond merely not causing trouble. This requires developing a robust personal system and capability set through accumulated experience.

我们应该去帮助那些值得帮助的人，而不是不加甄别地帮助所有人。

**[AI Synthesis]** This principle establishes a boundary condition for generosity, shifting the focus from mere activity to effective impact. The quality of the recipient and the potential for positive change must precede the commitment of resources.

**[Cognitive Shift Flag]** This contrasts with a purely egalitarian view of charity, suggesting that efficacy requires selectivity. This aligns with the concept of 'Value Exchange' discussed in other contexts.
