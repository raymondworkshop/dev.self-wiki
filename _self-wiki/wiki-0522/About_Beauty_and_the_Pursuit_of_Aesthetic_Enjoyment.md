> The appreciation of beauty requires a receptive mind capable of experiencing aesthetic pleasure, rather than needing to provide expert critical judgment. True creative work spans various domains—from the tangible arts like sculpture and woodworking to the abstract realms of philosophy and anthropology. Ultimately, the pursuit should be one of willing enjoyment rather than willing suffering.



### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/diary/philosophy/2020-06-10-about-beauty.md]]
## About Beauty

- **Experiencing Beauty**: The capacity to feel beauty is sufficient; expert judgment on good/bad value is secondary.
- **Creating Beauty**: Work exists in various forms: literature, music, visual arts; anthropology (different cultures/lives); philosophy (pure spiritual value).
- **Mindset**: The willingness to *enjoy* is more crucial than the willingness to *suffer*.

#### Photography & Visual Arts Practice
- **Data Visualization**: Infographics.
- **Practice Focus**: Line art, silhouette, color theory. Personal style leans toward simplicity and boldness rather than extreme delicacy.
- **Technical Progression (2021)**: Geometric form $\rightarrow$ Still Life $\rightarrow$ Plaster Cast $\rightarrow$ Portrait $\rightarrow$ Figure Study.
- **Compositional Elements (2021)**: Dynamic balance, contrast, hierarchy of subjects. Establishing relative scale between objects before finalizing placement.
- **Fundamentals**: Line, perspective, chiaroscuro (light/shadow). Sketching.

#### Foundational Practice Notes
- **Skill Refinement**: Need to practice basic skills (line, perspective, shading).
- **Observation**: Use reference materials to ground observation in reality.
