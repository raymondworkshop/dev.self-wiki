> This concept frames legal tax optimization not as avoidance, but as a fundamental duty of a responsible steward of one's own wealth. It elevates financial planning to a matter of personal integrity and duty.



### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/origin-apple-notes/weath.md]]
The core assertion is that managing one's wealth responsibly includes the duty of legal tax optimization. This shifts the perception of tax planning from mere compliance to an active, responsible stewardship of assets. (Source: [[origin-apple-notes/weath.md]])
