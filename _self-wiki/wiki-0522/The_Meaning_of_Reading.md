> Reading is not about accumulating facts, but about pursuing the essence of life, enriching experience, and observing life's patterns. True value lies in developing a unique perspective, aesthetic depth, and aligning intellectual pursuit with one's own interests.

### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/origin-apple-notes/读书不是为了知识，而是为了找到人生的真谛，丰富经验，观察人生的经验.md]]
**Core Thesis:** Reading serves as a journey toward discovering life's ultimate meaning, broadening experience, and observing life's patterns, rather than being a mere accumulation of facts.

**Critique of Superficiality:** Superficial engagement often lacks the necessary unique perspective, aesthetic quality, and spiritual depth required for profound insight. Such discussions can devolve into mere chance occurrences rather than inevitable truths.

**Pragmatic Alignment:** The pursuit must align with one's own interests and goals. Furthermore, discussions regarding this pursuit are most valuable when held with those whose interests are congruent with one's own.

**Ultimate Freedom:** Sustenance and survival are means to an end; true freedom is found when one has the choice regarding how to live and engage with the world.

### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/origin-apple-notes/三观 - 读书还不够多，不够深.md]]
The current intellectual foundation feels insufficient. To build a robust worldview (三观), the intake of knowledge must be both quantitatively broader and qualitatively deeper. This is a call to action regarding the pursuit of meaning through sustained, rigorous study.

### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/origin-apple-notes/reading.md]]
The reading of all good books is like a conversation with the finest minds of past centuries. This pursuit touches upon core concepts: Family, Wealth, Freedom, Ownership, and the maintenance of a peaceful mind and subconscious, alongside the right to defend these domains. (Source: [[origin-apple-notes/reading.md]])

### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/diary/softskills/2022-01-21-fictions-reading.md]]
The act of reading fiction involves a multi-stage cognitive process:

1. **Describe Events**: Establishing the narrative baseline.
2. **Sketch Details**: Capturing the granular texture of the experience.
3. **Find Allegory**: Distilling the narrative into its core symbolic meaning.
4. **Apply to Life**: Bridging the fictional gap to personal reality.
5. **Express Thoughts**: Articulating the resulting personal resonance.

This mirrors the necessary journey from raw input to distilled principle within the Self-Wiki framework.
