> The quality of your companions dictates the direction of your future endeavors. Therefore, choose your associates wisely, even when facing difficult circumstances, maintaining an optimistic outlook can spark innovation and novel solutions.

### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/origin-apple-notes/人工智能、能源及生物科学是好专业.md]]
**Core Insight: Companionship as Determinant**
The maxim, "人工智能、能源及生物科学是好专业," serves as a metaphor for the principle that the company you keep determines your future path. This underscores the necessity of making judicious choices regarding one's peers.

**Cognitive Application: Optimism in Adversity**
Even when circumstances are challenging, maintaining an optimistic disposition is a catalyst for innovation and the discovery of novel coping mechanisms. This mindset shift is crucial for navigating complex realities.

### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/origin-apple-notes/职场中女强人一样纵横股票市场，生活中却可以温柔相待.md]]
**Core Insight:** The ability to be fiercely competent and dominant in the professional sphere (e.g., navigating volatile stock markets) does not preclude, nor does it contradict, the capacity to offer tenderness and softness in personal relationships. 

**Synthesis:** This concept moves beyond a simple binary choice. It suggests that the same underlying drive, focus, and resilience that allows one to 'run the market' can be modulated and channeled into nurturing presence when the context shifts from competitive transaction to relational investment. 

**Cognitive Link:** This mirrors the tension between 'Transactional Value Exchange' in business and 'Relational Investment' in partnership. The skill is not in *having* the strength, but in *deploying* it appropriately.

### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/origin-apple-notes/我是一个喜欢做事的人，也喜欢独处，一个人多方便，不需要处理关系.md]]
**Core Tension Identified:** The individual exhibits a preference for autonomous action and the simplicity of solitude, viewing relationships as a variable requiring management. Yet, the underlying acknowledgment is that relationships hold significant, non-negotiable weight in the overall structure of existence.

**[AI Synthesis]:** This note captures a classic human paradox: the pull toward self-contained efficiency versus the pull toward relational depth. The current state is a cognitive mapping of this tension, not a resolution.

### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/diary/self/2015-08-16-work.md]]
**Ideal Work Attributes:**
*   **Foresight & Meaning:** The work must possess a promising future and hold intrinsic meaning.
*   **Leadership Alignment:** The superior must share similar values, possess competence, and be willing to invest in subordinates' growth.
*   **Autonomy & Respect:** A moderate level of control and freedom is required, alongside respect for the engineer's perspective.
*   **Compensation:** Compensation must be adequate.

**Ideal Life Attributes:**
*   **Freedom:** A degree of freedom (temporal, decisional, financial) is desired.
*   **Connection:** Presence of close family and friends.
*   **Authenticity:** Pursuing activities genuinely enjoyed.
*   **Contribution:** The ability to make a meaningful contribution to society within one's capacity.
