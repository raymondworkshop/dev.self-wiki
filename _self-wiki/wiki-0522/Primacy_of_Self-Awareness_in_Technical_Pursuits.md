> True mastery in technology and product development is contingent upon a robust foundation of self-understanding. Furthermore, the precision and depth of one's language remains a vital, parallel area requiring dedicated focus.



### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/origin-apple-notes/2021.md]]
## Core Directives

1. **Self-Awareness First:** Deep engagement with one's internal operating parameters must precede focused efforts on external technologies or products. This establishes the necessary cognitive ground truth.
2. **Linguistic Precision:** Dedicated focus on language acquisition and articulation is required. Language acts as the primary vector for translating complex internal models into actionable external reality.
