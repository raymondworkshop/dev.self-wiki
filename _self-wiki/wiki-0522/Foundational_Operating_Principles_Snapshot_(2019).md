> This document serves as a comprehensive, multi-domain blueprint outlining the author's goals across career, personal development, and relationships as of early 2019. It establishes the necessary conditions for holistic growth, demanding alignment between individual drive, professional execution, and relational values.

The core insight is the necessity of continuous, high-level self-cultivation across technical skill, leadership capacity, and emotional intelligence to achieve meaningful existence.



### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/diary/self/2019-02-21-new-year-challenge.md]]
## 🧠 Professional Trajectory & Mastery
- **Goal:** To lead a team in creating a world-class product, necessitating a shift from 'Senior Engineer' to 'Tech Leader' $\rightarrow$ 'PM' $\rightarrow$ 'Entrepreneur'.
- **Prerequisites:** Mastery of computer science fundamentals, ability to decompose large problems, and the capacity to drive one's own pace.
- **Skill Stack:** Deepening proficiency in programming languages (Python, Go, Java, Lisp) and rigorous research.
- **Alignment Principle:** The relationship between individual, company, and team must evolve from 'Wage Driver' to 'Visionary Partner' to 'Fateful Partner' (命运共同体).

## ❤️ Relational Dynamics (Partnership & Friendship)
- **Romantic Partner:** The relationship must be a mutual catalyst for becoming a better self, requiring shared values, emotional resonance ('feeling'), and mutual responsibility, not just pursuit of self.
- **Friendship:** Based on shared values and mutual benefit, moving beyond mere advice to practical application ('人所不欲，勿施于人').

## 🌱 Personal Cultivation & Lifestyle
- **Holistic Growth:** True growth requires simultaneous development in scholarship, character, temperament, and spirituality.
- **Action Items:** Establishing consistent routines (sleep, exercise, reading one original book per month) and improving soft skills (writing, presentation, storytelling).
- **Self-Awareness:** Deep understanding of one's strengths, weaknesses, and core values is paramount for contribution.
