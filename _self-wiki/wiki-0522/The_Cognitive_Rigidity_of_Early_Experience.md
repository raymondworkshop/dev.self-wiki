> Early life experiences possess the power to calcify into limiting cognitive frameworks, creating a resistance to novel thought. To maintain cognitive agility and prevent intellectual stagnation, it is crucial to intentionally seek out perspectives from those whose life stages differ significantly from one's own.



### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/origin-apple-notes/人真的会被少年经验困住; 多和 年轻人交流.md]]
The core insight drawn from the source material is that the accumulation of experiences, while forming identity, can also create a feedback loop that reinforces outdated models. This rigidity is most pronounced when the foundational experiences are those of youth. The antidote is not merely exposure, but active, high-signal exchange with individuals whose operating parameters differ from one's own, particularly those in earlier life stages, to introduce necessary entropy and challenge entrenched assumptions. (Source: [[origin-apple-notes/人真的会被少年经验困住; 多和 年轻人交流.md]])
