> The core realization from this difficult day is the primacy of familial bonds over professional pursuits. This painful experience forces a re-evaluation of where true, foundational value lies in life.

### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/origin-apple-notes/今天是个分手和伤心的日子.md]]
**Principle Distilled:** 家庭大于事业 (Family > Career).

This entry serves as a critical, emotionally charged data point forcing a recalibration of life priorities. The pain of the breakup serves as the catalyst for affirming the foundational value of familial connection over professional ambition. (Source: [[origin-apple-notes/今天是个分手和伤心的日子.md]])

### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/origin-apple-notes/人，在精不在多.md]]
**Principle of Deep Connection:** Genuine friendship is found in shared pursuits where both parties possess some level of competence and are willing to exchange ideas. This mutual contribution elevates the relationship beyond mere companionship.

**Application:** While casual acquaintances are fine, the most rewarding relationships are those that function as intellectual sparring partners, allowing for mutual growth and the alleviation of isolation through shared thought.

**Contrast:** The value of a relationship is not measured by quantity but by the quality and depth of the intellectual and experiential exchange it facilitates.

**(Source: [[origin-apple-notes/人，在精不在多.md]])**

### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/origin-apple-notes/life  career.md]]
**有难度的乐趣 (The Joy of Difficulty)**
- A deep, sustained obsession with a career or hobby.
- Requires accumulation of time and mental/physical space.
- This is the authentic passion that cannot be feigned.

**对于人也是 (It is also for people)**
- Natural acceptance of the human condition.
- A difficult dream involves curiosity about people and a chosen embrace of this imperfect world.

**Synthesis Point:** The acceptance of life's inherent difficulty and imperfection is a prerequisite for finding deep meaning in both vocation and connection. (Source: [[origin-apple-notes/life career.md]])

### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/origin-apple-notes/career softskills.md]]
**Collaborative Growth:** Seek partners committed to mutual support and shared advancement. Success in group settings often comes from being the one to break stalemates or propose viable solutions when discussions stall.
**Navigating Upward Relationships:** When advising superiors, provide comprehensive, honest counsel, but refrain from making the final decision for them. Recognize that interpersonal dynamics act as a catalyst, but performance dictates the outcome.
**Establishing External Presence:** Beyond executing one's core duties, actively build an external professional image within the industry. Aim to become the recognized expert in a specific niche, making your expertise the default thought when others seek counsel in that domain. (Source: [[origin-apple-notes/career softskills.md]])

### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/diary/notes/2023-06-13-on-managing-yourself.md]]
**Metrics for a Measured Life:**
*   **Purpose:** A clear, transcendent purpose must be actively cultivated (e.g., dedicated time for reflection on existence).
*   **Career Fulfillment:** Happiness stems from the triad of learning, assuming responsibility, and achieving recognized contributions.
*   **Family Bonds:** Enduring happiness in relationships requires that the shared purpose remains central to how time, talents, and energy are spent.
*   **Personal Motivations:** Core drives include being present with loved ones, pursuing exciting/impactful endeavors, and building a long-term entrepreneurial vision to enact global change.

(Source: [[diary/notes/2023-06-13-on-managing-yourself.md]])
