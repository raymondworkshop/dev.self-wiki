### Distillation (2026-05-20) - source: [[../raw/origin-apple-notes/一边厌烦了平淡，一边厌烦了别人的模仿.md]]
The feeling described—being annoyed by both the mundane and the imitation of others—is a powerful indicator of a high standard for existence. This tension suggests that the pursuit of originality is not merely a preference but a fundamental operating principle. 

**Core Insight:** The drive toward unique expression is inextricably linked to the rejection of comfortable conformity. To maintain this standard, one must cultivate a robust internal compass capable of discerning genuine depth from superficiality.

**Cognitive Link:** This resonates with the tension between 'Clarity vs. Complexity in Thought Articulation' and the need for 'Principles of Self-Awareness and Influence.'
