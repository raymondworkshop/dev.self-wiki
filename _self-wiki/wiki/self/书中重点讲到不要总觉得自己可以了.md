# 书中重点讲到不要总觉得自己可以了

书中强调了切忌自满，必须保持谦逊的心态，如同白纸一般，不断学习和提升。积极获取他人的反馈是实现持续进步的关键。

## Evolution

This thought has evolved from a general caution against arrogance into a structured methodology for growth, contrasting with the tendency to feel oneself is always sufficient.
