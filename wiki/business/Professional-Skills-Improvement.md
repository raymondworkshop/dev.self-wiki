Cognitive improvement is based on making precise judgments through training one's thinking patterns and gaining experience by solving difficult problems in real-world situations. This page outlines strategies for self-development, soft skills, decision-making, and professional growth.

## Core Philosophies
- **Cognitive Development**: Learn, think, apply, and then review. Reduce the cycle time to improve outcomes.
- **Problem Solving**: Engage in "seeing yourself, seeing the world, and seeing all living beings" to upgrade your mindset.
- **Strategic Thinking**: Focus on "why" and "how" rather than just "what."

## Soft Skills & Decision Making
- **Social Interaction**: Be warm and approachable. Focus on finding common ground rather than debating.
- **Communication**: Listen first, seek to understand, and provide value before asking for favors.
- **Decision-Making**: Define the goal, guiding principles, and criteria before evaluating solutions.

## Professional Growth
- **Business Strategy**: Learn by doing. Adapt to your environment rather than just competing on strength. Find your ecological niche.
- **Feedback**: Seek feedback actively, focusing on how your experience matches the organization's needs.
