A structured daily rhythm, combined with specific dietary and exercise habits, is proposed to maintain high cognitive function and energy levels. This structure balances deep work periods with necessary physical and mental decompression.
## Evolution
The initial notes on energy focused heavily on sleep and exercise variables (raw/origin-apple-notes/energy.md). This has been integrated into a comprehensive, time-blocked lifestyle schedule, adding dietary specifics and activity timing to create a holistic operational framework (raw/origin-apple-notes/lifestyle.md).
## Key Concepts
*   **Morning Protocol**: Wake up around 8:00 am, address emails, and engage in initial thinking/writing.
*   **Work Blocks**: Structured deep work sessions (e.g., 10:00 am - 11:30 am) followed by a midday break.
*   **Recovery**: Lunch should involve stepping away from the office to allow the brain to rest and process information elsewhere.
*   **Energy Management**: Sleep targets are 8-9 hours; caffeine intake must cease by noon to prevent disruption.
