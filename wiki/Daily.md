Daily life management involves maintaining productive habits, managing logistics like travel and documentation, and ensuring operational efficiency through clear methodology and time-awareness.

### Methodology and Mindset
- **Fake It Till You Make It**: When confidence or skill is lacking, adopt the persona of someone who has it. Action often precedes feeling; by "faking" the behavior, you eventually internalize the state.
- **Ready is an Illusion**: No one is ever truly "ready." We simply become "less unprepared." Waiting for perfect readiness is a form of procrastination. Action must be taken in the face of uncertainty.
- **Time Cost Analysis**: Before committing to any task, calculate the time cost (先算时间成本). Time and peace of mind are the most expensive modes of payment.

### Habits and Personal Systems

#### Daily Learning and Mastery
- **Shadowing Practice**: Engage in daily language shadowing (Shadowing on a daily basis). Follow speech simultaneously to internalize rhythm and prosody.
- **Mastery through Repetition**: Use "experience-based" learning—knowledge comes from direct practice and observation rather than just theory.

#### Physical Presentation and Health
- **Start Your Day Before the SUN**: Waking up early (before the sun) provides a psychological "win" and ensures quiet time for focused work or reflection.
- **Sleep Regularity**: Maintain a consistent wake-up time regardless of when you went to bed. This stabilizes the circadian rhythm more effectively than varying wake times.
- **Dress Well**: Professional and intentional dressing (dress well) as a form of self-respect and confidence building.
- **Physical Fitness**: Maintain a fit body through regular exercise. Physical health is a foundational component of confidence.
- **Emotional Health**: Master emotions by writing them down (辨真伪). Identify which feelings require action and which are "inner storytelling" to be ignored.

#### Meta-Awareness Habits
- **Helicopter View**: Use the "直升机视角" during interactions to observe the situation from above and align with high-level goals.
- **Awareness of "Inner Drama"**: Catch yourself when creating stories in your head and stop them immediately (叫停止损).
- **Intention First**: Start every action and conversation by clarifying the intention (Intention is all).

### Logistics and Practical Management

#### Decision Making
- **Reducing Friction**: Minimize thinking for repetitive tasks. Regularize and simplify life as much as possible.
- **Seeking Responsibility**: Seek the burden of decision. Maturity is measured by the ability to take charge of one's life.

#### Documentation and Travel
- **Passports and IDs**: Maintain accessible records of important documents.
- **Driving (HK)**:
    - **Preparation**: Strict adherence to safety checks (mirrors, blind spots).
    - **Technique**: Maintain defensive driving distances and tactical braking in narrow lanes.

#### Digital and Financial
- **Security**: Centralized and secure management of passwords and credentials.
- **Wealth Strategy**: Position yourself where performance is measurable and decisions have high leverage.
