Daily life management involves maintaining productive habits, managing logistics like travel and documentation, and ensuring operational efficiency in practical matters.

### Habits and Personal Systems

#### Productivity and Well-being
- **Work on Yourself**: Prioritize personal growth before helping others.
- **Sleep and Energy**: Early to bed, early to rise. Good health is maintained through consistent eating habits and exercise.
- **Calmness and Focus**: Practice meditation and mindfulness to achieve mental clarity.
- **Freedom**: True freedom is control over your time; ensure your schedule does not control you.
- **Listening**: Focus on active listening during daily interactions.

#### Decision Making
- **Reducing Friction**: Minimize thinking for repetitive tasks. Regularize and simplify life as much as possible to save mental energy for important decisions.
- **Seeking Responsibility**: The core difference in maturity is seeking the burden of decision rather than seeking relief from it.

### Logistics and Practical Management

#### Documentation and Travel
- **Passports and IDs**: Maintain records of file numbers and appointment times (e.g., Passport File: 2018 0517 0463 022).
- **Driving (HK)**:
    - **Preparation**: Check mirrors (rear, left, right) before starting and stopping.
    - **Technique**: Maintain two car lengths' distance. Use "Tactical Braking" when encountering oncoming traffic in narrow lanes.
    - **Safety**: Always yield to oncoming traffic when turning right. Overlook death traps (blind spots) before turning or lane changing.
- **Addresses**: Maintain a record of important addresses (e.g., Nanshan, Shenzhen for 范志飞).

#### Digital and Financial
- **Security**: Maintain records of passwords and WiFi credentials (e.g., WiFi: 19969169).
- **Wealth Strategy**: To build wealth, position yourself where performance is measurable and decisions have high leverage.
- **Shopping**: Keep running lists for essentials like food (garlic, instant noodles) and stationery.

### Travel and Exploration

#### Shenzhen Trips
- **Preparation**: Use WeChat for health declarations. Ensure Home Return Permit and Shenzhen Tong (or RMB) are ready.
- **Routes**: Upper Shui -> Lo Wu. Explore areas like Dafen Oil Painting Village and Dameisha Seashore Walkway (8km hike).
- **Dining**: Prefer local spots like Wood House BBQ or seafood at Yantian.

#### Local Exploration (HK)
- **Art and Culture**: Visit murals and street art (e.g., HKWALLS in Central).

### Miscellaneous Notes
- **Blockchain**: Explore blockchain technology as a potential project area.
- **Partnerships**: Look for business partners with experience in online business (books, travel, medicine).
