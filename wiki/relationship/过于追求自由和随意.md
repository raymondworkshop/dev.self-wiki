# 过于追求自由和随意

过度追求绝对的自由和随性，往往伴随着对被限制的恐惧。这种恐惧可能阻碍了在现实世界中进行有效的人际交往和协作的能力。

## Evolution
This observation appears to be a reflection on the balance between individual autonomy and the requirements of group interaction, a theme that may relate to the constraints discussed in [[insight work]].
