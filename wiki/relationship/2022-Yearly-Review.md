2022 was a year of profound reflection on love-strength, self-awareness, and navigating relationships. This page synthesizes insights from weekly check-ins and yearly reviews.

## Key Reflections
- **Love-strength**: Self-reliance derived from knowing who you are uniquely. It is abundant and leads to curiosity.
- **Relationships**: Build relationships through pressure testing and clear boundaries. Treat family with respect and care.
- **Business/Career**: Focus on cash flow, understanding real human needs, and maintaining professional integrity.

## Lessons Learned
- **Emotions**: Acknowledge feelings without letting them control behavior. Use inner self-dialogue to prioritize goals.
- **Growth**: Treat challenges as opportunities to cultivate strengths. Value "why" and "how" over simple solutions.
