Health is a holistic pursuit involving clean eating, consistent physical training, and a lifestyle that promotes well-being and social connection.

### Nutrition and Diet
- **Healthy Eating**: Focus on healthy foods and avoid smoking.
- **Consistency**: Maintain good eating habits as a foundation for physical health.
- **Specifics**: Notes on specific foods like "Pepperonishk."

### Fitness and Gym Training

#### Weight Training
- **Back**: Lat Pulldowns (focus on scapular depression, 10 reps x 4 sets).
- **Chest**: Flat Bench Dumbbell Press. Focus on stability, tight core, and controlled movement.
- **Arms**: 
    - **Triceps**: Overhead Dumbbell Extensions (maintain alignment of shoulder, elbow, and wrist).
    - **Biceps**: Targeted dumbbell curls.
- **Core**: Abs and "mermaid lines" (obliques), including sit-ups.

#### Cardiovascular and Outdoor
- **Running**: Regular running for cardiovascular health.
- **Hiking (HK)**: 
    - Tai Tam (大潭)
    - Tai Mei Tuk (大尾督) - Cycling
    - Tai Po Kau Nature Reserve (大埔滘林徑藍路)
- **Philosophy**: Hiking and outdoor activities should focus on "comfort and happiness" (舒服和开心) and are seen as "valuable works" in themselves.

### Lifestyle and Social Fitness
- **Habit Formation**: Create lifestyle habits that integrate social connection (e.g., gym buddies, catching local events).
- **Consistency**: The key to both physical and relational health is consistency.
- **Emotional Uplift**: Goals for activities should include both parties feeling better and having uplifted spirits.
